

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Security control and transfer of personal data of agency employees (EMONA KRONI s.r.o. company)



On the basis of the Office's control plan, the Office carried out a security check of personal data of agency employees and their transfer to clients of the controlled person. The controlled person processes the personal data of job applicants and agency employees in paper and electronic form on the basis of an employment contract or an agreement on work activities concluded with them for the purpose of arranging employment with a third party (employer).

The controlled person performs his activities on the basis of special laws, in particular ยง 14 paragraph 1 letter a), b) and c) of Act No. 435/2004 Coll., on employment, to the extent of permits issued by the Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs and the Labor Office of the Czech Republic.

In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

The inspection was conducted by the inspector PhDr. Petr Krejci.
