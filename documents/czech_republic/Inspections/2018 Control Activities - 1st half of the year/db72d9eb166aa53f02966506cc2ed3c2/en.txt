

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity of Continental Automotive Czech Republic s.r.o. - processing of personal data of employees during the control of their work activities



On the basis of the Office's control plan, the Office carried out an inspection of the scope and method of processing employees' personal data in connection with the inspection of their work activities, following the conditions set forth in § 316 of Act No. 262/2006 Coll., Labor Code.

The office focused on the processing of personal data as part of personnel management, when managing smart cards, when ensuring the entry mode, when operating the camera system, when using IT technology and the GPS system, and when protecting premises and production.

The Office checked the fulfillment of the obligations arising in particular from Section 5, paragraph 1 (obligations of the controller) and Section 2 (legal titles for processing), Section 6 (contract for processing), Section 11 (obligation to provide information), Section 12 (data subject access to information ), § 13 (security of personal data) and § 21 (access to data, right to explanation and correction) of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

The inspection was conducted by the inspector MVDr. František Bartoš.
