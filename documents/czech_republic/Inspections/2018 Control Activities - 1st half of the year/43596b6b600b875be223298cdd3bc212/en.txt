

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Control of transfer of personal data to other entities (Volleyball club Jirkov, z.s.)



On the basis of the initiative, the Office carried out an inspection of the association, the subject of which was the handover of a list of 55 children, or members of the controlled person, who are pupils of the first to third grade of elementary school, of the company Broker Consulting, a.s. The list contained the surnames and first names of the minors, the class they attend, and the contact (phone number and e-mail address) of the minors or their legal representatives.

The subject action was taken by the objected person in order to obtain funds for its operation, as it was supposed to receive commissions from the business and services provided by Broker Consulting, a.s., in causal connection with the subject list. The Office found a violation of ยง 5 paragraph 2 (legal title for processing) and ยง 13 paragraph 1 of Act No. 101/2000 Coll. (taking security measures). Furthermore, the Office found a violation of ยง 13 paragraph 4 letter c) Act No. 101/2000 Coll. (logging of operations and records of accesses).

Considering that the inspected person immediately corrected the defective condition, the Office did not impose corrective measures. The office imposed a fine of CZK 8,000 on the inspected person.

The inspection was conducted by the inspector PhDr. Petr Krejci.
