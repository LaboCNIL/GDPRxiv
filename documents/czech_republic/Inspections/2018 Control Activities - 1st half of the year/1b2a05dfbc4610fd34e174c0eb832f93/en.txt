The inspection, the subject of which was the processing of personal data through the camera system located in the apartment building, was ordered after a previous inspection that took place in 2017.

Even repeated checks did not reveal a violation of the law. The Office found that the controlled person fulfilled the information obligation not only before the start of the processing, but also fulfilled it during the processing (by informing the members of the controlled person at the meeting of the community of owners and dropping the information into mailboxes) and still fulfills it (by posting information signs).

In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

The inspection was conducted by the inspector PhDr. Petr Krejci.
