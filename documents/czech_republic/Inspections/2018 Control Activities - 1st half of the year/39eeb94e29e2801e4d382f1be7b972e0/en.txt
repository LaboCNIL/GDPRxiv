On the basis of the Office's control plan, the Office conducted a comprehensive review of the processing of personal data at a company that operates a chain of stores, both of the personal data of employees and customers of this company.

The following were selected for detailed inspection: a camera system with recording, a database of electronic entry cards and a database of harmful events.

The Office focused on the procedures of the inspected person in securing personal data in the mentioned systems and evaluated the technical and organizational measures taken as sufficient. The controlled person also fulfills information obligations and complies with obligations in the area of data subjects' right to access personal data, including the right to erasure and disposal of personal data. In accordance with § 6 of Act No. 101/2000 Coll., the inspected person has concluded contracts with processors of personal data.

In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

The inspection was conducted by inspector PaeDr. Jana Rybínová.
