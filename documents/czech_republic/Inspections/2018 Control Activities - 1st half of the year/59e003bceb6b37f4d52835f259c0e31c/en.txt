Based on the initiative, the Office carried out an inspection of the processing of personal data of the bank's clients, focusing on the disclosure of personal data within the framework of bank payment systems.

The complainant stated that his personal data, including name, surname, address, account number and amount, are made available to the recipient of the payment on the account statement, based on incorrectly set software. However, he did not substantiate his claim. During the inspection, the Office did not find any information that would support the claim of the complainant.

In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.

The inspection was conducted by the inspector MVDr. František Bartoš.
