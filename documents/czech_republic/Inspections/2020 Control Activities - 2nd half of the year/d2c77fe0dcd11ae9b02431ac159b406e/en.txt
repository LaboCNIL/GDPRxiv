Company


The inspection was carried out on the basis of the Office's inspection plan for 2020. The inspection found that the legal title for the processing of personal data of customers and potential customers is primarily a contract in the sense of Act No. 89/2012 Coll., Civil Code. The processing of personal data is necessary for the fulfillment of contracts between the controlled person and its customers in the sense of Article 6 paragraph 1 letter b) of the general regulation. Another legal title for the processing of personal data is also Article 6 paragraph 1 letter c) and f) of the general regulation, when personal data is processed also if the processing is necessary for the fulfillment of the controller's legal obligations (the area of tax administration, accounting, payroll obligations, however, this processing does not apply to the processing of personal data of customers of the controlled entity, i.e. the subject of the control) and, last but not least, personal data is processed for the purpose of offering previously provided services (direct marketing), for the prevention of fraud or ensuring the security of the network and information of IT systems, for possible legal disputes, here the legal title of the processing is the legitimate interest of the administrator according to Article 6 paragraph 1 letter f) of the general regulation. The inspection found that the inspected person processes personal data of clients in accordance with the legal title in the sense of Article 6, paragraph 1 letter b), or letter c) and f) of the general regulation. The inspection did not reveal a violation in any of the eight inspection findings of the above-cited articles of the general regulation. The inspected person did not file any objections to the inspection finding.
