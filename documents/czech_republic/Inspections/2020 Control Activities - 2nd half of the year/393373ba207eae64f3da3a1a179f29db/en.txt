

Path: Home page > Main menu > Surveillance and decision-making activity Cameras in public space (UOOU-00811/20)
Operator of television/internet broadcasting


The inspection was initiated on the basis of complaints in which the processing of personal data was objected to by means of a camera occupying the area of the railway crossing in the Prague-Řeporyje district, the recording of which is published in real time on the website www.mall.tv and parts of the recordings are also published within program Extreme Mayor, or taken over by other news media (crossing by female teachers with children when the barriers were down). Before starting the inspection, the inspectors discovered that on the www.mall.tv website, in addition to the recording from the railway crossing in Řeporyje, there is also a recording from a camera that captures the area around the Vlasov monument on Řeporyjské náměstí in the Prague-Řeporyje district. The Office launched an inspection, the subject of which was compliance with the obligations set out in the General Regulation and Act No. 110/2019 Coll., on the processing of personal data, in particular the legality of the processing of personal data in connection with the operation of the cameras in question. processed personal data from the aforementioned cameras without a legal title. Given that the auditee removed both cameras during the inspection and removed the recordings from these cameras from its website, the Office did not impose corrective measures on the auditee.
