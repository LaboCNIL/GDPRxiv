

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Compliance with the principles of personal data processing in the BACHELOR system (UOOU-02491/20)
Elementary School


The subject of the control was compliance with the general regulation in the BACHELOR system with a focus on the principles of personal data processing according to Article 5, the legality of personal data processing according to Article 6, the conditions for expressing consent according to Article 7, the legality of processing special categories of personal data according to Article 9, information the administrator's obligation according to Articles 12 and 13, compliance with the rights of data subjects according to Articles 15-23, security of personal data according to Article 32, including the obligations of suppliers of the BACHELOR system and individual applications. The inspection was initiated on the basis of the Office's inspection plan for 2020. The inspection revealed that the inspected person processes personal data in the BAKALÁŘI system in particular in accordance with § 28 of Act No. 561/2004 Coll., the Education Act (Article 6, paragraph 1, letter c ) of the general regulation), on the basis of legitimate interest (contact details), with the consent of the legal representative (health insurance), for the period determined by Act No. 499/2004 Coll., on archiving and file service. The controlled person informs the legal representatives of pupils about the processing of personal data in the sense of Article 12 of the General Regulation on its website in the document Information on the processing of personal data, in which the rights of data subjects are also detailed. The system provider is BAKALÁŘI software s.r.o., with which the controlled person has a license agreement. The contract also includes the protection of personal data. The inspection carried out did not reveal a violation of the inspected provisions of the general regulation.
