On the basis of 25 initiatives, the Office carried out an inspection regarding compliance with Act No. 480/2004 Coll.



He found that the controlled person violated the obligations set out in § 7 paragraph 2 of Act No. 480/2004 Coll., i.e. to use electronic contact details for the purpose of disseminating business messages by electronic means only in relation to users who have given prior consent. In addition, the controlled person in several cases violated § 7 paragraph 4 letter a) and b) of Act No. 480/2004 Coll., because the disseminated commercial messages were not clearly and clearly marked as commercial messages and did not contain the identification of the sender.



The office imposed a fine of CZK 117,000 on the inspected person.


The inspection was conducted by inspector Ing. Josef Vacula.
