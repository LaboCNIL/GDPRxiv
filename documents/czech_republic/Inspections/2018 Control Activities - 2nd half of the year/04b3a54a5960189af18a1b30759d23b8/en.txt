

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Control of processing of personal data by the employment agency (PRAGMA personální agentura s.r.o.)



On the basis of its inspection plan, the Office carried out an inspection in accordance with Act No. 101/2000 Coll., the subject of which was the processing of personal data by employment agencies with regard to the tightening of the conditions of employment agencies following the amendment of Act No. 435/004 Coll. about employment.



The inspection proved that the inspected person mediates employment within the meaning of § 14 paragraph 1 letter a), b) and c) of the Employment Act. During this activity, it processes the personal data of agency employees, to the extent necessary for the activity according to the permits issued by the Labor Office of the Czech Republic. Personal data is processed in paper and electronic form for the purpose of arranging employment with a third party, a client of a recruitment agency.



The Office warned the controlled person of possible risks in this processing of personal data and recommended suitable procedures for minimizing these risks. However, in connection with the subject processing of personal data, he did not find a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.


The inspection was conducted by the inspector PhDr. Petr Krejci.
