

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Control of personal data processing through the camera system in the cafe (natural person running a business)



Based on the initiative, the Office carried out an inspection in accordance with Act No. 101/2000 Coll., the subject of which was the processing of personal data through a camera system with recording equipment in the premises of the cafe.



The inspection revealed that the inspected person processes personal data through one camera in connection with the operation of his cafe. This camera system specifically monitors the cash register and the entrance door of the cafe. In the past, there was a burglary attempt and intentional non-payment of expenses by a group of people, which was proven to them by the Police of the Czech Republic through a recording from this camera system. Following the inspection findings, the Office came to the conclusion that the inspected person has a legal title for this processing of personal data.



In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.


The inspection was conducted by the inspector PhDr. Petr Krejci.
