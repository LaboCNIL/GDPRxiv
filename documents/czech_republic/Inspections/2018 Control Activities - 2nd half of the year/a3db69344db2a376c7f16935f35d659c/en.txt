Based on the initiative, the Office carried out an inspection in accordance with Act No. 101/2000 Coll., the subject of which was the handling of contact data of former employees processed through the mail server and published on the website of the inspected person.



The inspection focused on the fulfillment of obligations according to § 5, paragraph 1 (in particular, the scope of processed data, the period of their retention, ensuring their accuracy, processing only in accordance with the purpose for which they were collected), § 5, paragraph 2 (legal title for processing) and § 13 paragraphs 1 and 2 (security of personal data) and § 21 of the Act (access to data, right to explanation and correction) No. 101/2000 Coll.



In connection with the subject processing of personal data, the Office did not detect a violation of Act No. 101/2000 Coll.


The inspection was conducted by inspector PaeDr. Jana Rybínová.
