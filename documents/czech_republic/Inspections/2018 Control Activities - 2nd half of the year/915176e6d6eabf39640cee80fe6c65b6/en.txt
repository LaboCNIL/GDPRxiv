

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Control of the implementation of the right to erasure in the environment of internet search engines (the company Seznam.cz, a.s.)



On the basis of its inspection plan, the Office carried out an inspection according to Regulation (EU) 2016/679, the subject of which was the realization of the right to erasure, or the right to be forgotten in the environment of Internet search engines. As part of the inspection, he also checked two related initiatives that he received.



The inspection proved that the general procedure of the inspected person in fulfilling the obligation according to Article 17 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (right to erasure) is in accordance with legal regulations. The inspectors also evaluated the specific procedure in relation to the two complainants as a procedure corresponding to the requirements of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.



Therefore, the Office did not detect a violation of legal regulations in connection with the processing of personal data in question.


Inspector Ing Josef Vacula managed the inspection.
