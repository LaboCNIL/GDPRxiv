A company operating an online store


A company operating an online store
uses cookies within its website, while control
was initiated on the basis of a complaint directed against the processing of personal data
data in connection with the use of these files.

The inspection was focused on the setting
company and compliance with the obligations established by the general regulation (in particular
then the legal title for the processing of personal data according to Article 6, compliance
conditions for expressing consent according to Article 7 and fulfillment of obligations according to Articles 12 to 13)
in connection with the use of cookies on the website of the Internet
trade.

The Office stated that the company violated the obligation set out in Article 6
paragraph 1 letter a) of the general regulation, because she was not there for an unspecified period of time
the condition of expressing informed consent is met. She committed the violation by
that if the user decided to obtain more information about
processing of personal data and clicked on the "Personal data" link, thereby simultaneously granting
uninformed consent to the processing of personal data through cookies. The company has corrected the problem
a state where it is currently no longer possible to grant consent to processing
personal data via cookies by simply clicking on any link to
page. The inspectors also focused on fulfillment
the obligations specified in the provision of ArticleÂ 12 of the General Regulation in connection with s
by providing information, within the scope of Article 13 of this regulation. Listed
provisions govern the controller's obligation to inform the data subject of all
essential parameters of personal data processing, including his rights, which
arise in relation to this processing.

The company did not violate the stated obligations, that is
fulfilled the information obligation according to the general regulation.
