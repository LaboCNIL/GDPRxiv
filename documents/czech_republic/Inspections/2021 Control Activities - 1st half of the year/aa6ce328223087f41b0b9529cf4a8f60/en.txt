Kindergarten


The reason for the inspection was the initiative of one of the parents
of a child attending a multi-class kindergarten that runs two others
kindergartens as their separate workplaces. The processing of personal data was objected to
data through a camera occupying the area of the dressing room where the children are changing.

The office started an inspection, the subject of which was
compliance with the obligations established by the general regulation and Act No. 110/2019 Coll.,
on the processing of personal data, in particular the control of the legality of the processing
personal data in connection with the operation of the camera in question.

The inspectors found that the kindergarten by
has placed and operates cameras in locker room areas designated for
changing children's clothes, violated the personal data controller's obligation to process personal data
data on the basis of a legal title according to Article 6 paragraph 1 letter a) to f) general
ordinance. By the fact that the kindergarten processed the personal data of the children, their parents,
respectively of persons recorded by the camera system without a legal title, violated
the principle of legality established in Article 5 paragraph 1 letter a) of this regulation.

The kindergarten removed the cameras from all changing rooms during the inspection
intended for changing children's clothes in all the buildings it operates, that's why it wasn't
Proceedings on the imposition of remedial measures were conducted.
