Owners Associations


The inspection was carried out on the basis of a complaint,
when, according to the complainant, he should have been without a legal title and without proper security
of recorded footage, a camera system is operated in the apartment building
with cameras especially at the main entrance to the house and at the door to the cellars
space. Â¨The authority started an inspection,
the subject of which was compliance with the obligations established by general regulation a
the Act on the Processing of Personal Data, in particular the control of the legality of the processing
personal data in connection with the operation of the camera system. The inspectors found that
the community of owners set a purpose and
means of processing personal data, decided on the acquisition, installation and
use of a camera system with recording. The purpose of the processing is the fulfillment of legal obligations in the management of the house and
of land in accordance with the statutes of the community of owners based on the decision of the meeting of the assembly
owners following previous incidents at the home. It also stipulated
the period of processing of personal data, including the method of their disposal, conditions
operation of the camera system, including its security, camera system administrator
and determined the conditions under which the captured record can be viewed and under what conditions
conditions it can be used.

Furthermore, it was found that the community
owners fulfill their obligations according to Articles 12 to 14 of the general regulation
through written Information about
processing of personal data in connection with the operation of the camera
system with a record, which is placed on a bulletin board next to the entrance to the house and
and information tables that are located near each one
from installed cameras.

By checking
no violation of the general regulation was found.
