making information (personal data documents) available through
remote access whiteboards that present more risks than the
classic official boards.

obligations under the general regulation on publication
documents, in particular: method of anonymization, legal title for posting
and hanging, considering the consequences of hanging caused by search engines on
internet, profiling of data obtained within
so-called monitoring of official boards, data posting time, data archiving and access to them.
