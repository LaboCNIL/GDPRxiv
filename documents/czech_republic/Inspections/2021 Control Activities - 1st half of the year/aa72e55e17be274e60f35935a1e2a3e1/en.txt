School


The inspection was carried out in accordance with the inspection
the plan to which the controlled person was enrolled in connection with s
the "Safe School" project. Part of it should have been utilization
biometric identification of pupils during their registration when entering the school building. Due to the cancellation of the use of this
part of the project was not confirmed to be audited during the audit
the person processed data in connection with the use of biometrics
identification of pupils, as originally intended by the founder of the school.

It was not found that even in context
with participation in the "STOP violence at schools - crime prevention" project,
on the basis of which efforts to increase safety at the school were due
camera systems were purchased, the general regulation or Act No. 110/2019 Coll., on the processing of personal data, was violated. AT
however, the number of controlled persons was reduced by dismantling 11 cameras
of them and limiting footage from existing cameras on the base
of the evaluation opinion drawn up following Instructions 3/2019 to
processing of personal data through video technology issued by the European
choir.
