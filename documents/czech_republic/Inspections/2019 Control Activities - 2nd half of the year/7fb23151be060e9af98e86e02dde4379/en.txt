Transport company


The control was carried out on the basis of the Control Plan for 2019. The subject of the control was the processing of personal data of passengers, whose personal data were obtained both in connection with their request for the issuance of a travel document, and also from the passenger's identity card during the transport control, if the passenger does not present a valid travel document document and does not pay the fare and fare surcharge on the spot. The legal obligations of the controller were checked, in particular according to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 and Act No. 110/2019 Coll., on the processing of personal data. It was not found that the controlled person processed personal data to a different extent and for a period necessary for the purpose of processing, or did not fulfill the administrator's other obligations, especially towards data subjects. Additional information: In the event that the passenger does not present a valid travel document at the request of the authorized person during the transport inspection, he is obliged to pay the fare and a fare surcharge, or to present himself with a personal document and provide the necessary personal data for the recovery of fares and fare surcharges; personal data required for the enforcement of fare payment and fare surcharges means first name, surname, date and place of birth, address for delivery and personal document number [ยง 18a para. 2 lit. c) of Act No. 111/1994 Coll., on road transport].
