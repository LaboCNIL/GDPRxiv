Based on the Office's Inspection Plan for 2019, the Office conducted an inspection, the subject of which was the processing of personal data through mobile applications developed by the inspected person. The check revealed that the processing of personal data is lawful, based on the consent of the data subjects according to Article 6 point 1, letter a) Regulation (EU) 2016/679 and at the same time the data subject controlled in the Personal Data Protection Principles is informed of all his rights according to Articles 13 â€“ 17 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679. Furthermore, the inspection found that the inspected person took appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure an adequate level of personal data security together with another processor. The inspection did not reveal a violation of the established obligations.
