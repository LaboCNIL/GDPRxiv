On the basis of 159 initiatives, the Office at the company MJ VENTURES s.r.o. control regarding compliance with Act No. 480/2004 Coll.

The Office found a violation of Section 7, Paragraph 2 of Act No. 480/2004 Coll., as the addressees of commercial communications did not give prior consent to the use of their electronic contact details for the purpose of disseminating commercial communications. Furthermore, the Office found a violation of ยง 7 paragraph 4 letter b) of Act No. 480/2004 Coll., as the sent messages did not contain any identification of the sender, and a violation of ยง 7 paragraph 4 letter c) of Act No. 480/2004 Coll., as commercial communications were sent without a valid address to which the addressee could directly and effectively send information that he does not wish the sender to continue sending commercial information.

The Chairperson of the Office rejected the objections submitted by the inspected person against the inspection findings. In the matter, the Office initiated proceedings on a misdemeanor.

The inspection was conducted by inspector Ing. Josef Vacula.
