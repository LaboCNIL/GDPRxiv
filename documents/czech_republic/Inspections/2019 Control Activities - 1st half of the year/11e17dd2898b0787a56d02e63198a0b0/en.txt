Based on the complaint, the Office carried out an inspection, the subject of which was the security of personal data during its processing by the assessment committee in connection with the assessment of the degree of disability by the district social security administration. data) made available to an unauthorized person. The Authority found that this disclosure had indeed taken place. However, the inspectors discovered that this was not a systemic error by the inspected person, but an isolated error by the medical examiner. This doctor, although properly familiar with the internal regulations, mistakenly gave the opinion to another client of the district social security administration. Based on its investigation, the Czech Social Security Administration punished the doctor in question by issuing a reprimand and reducing his remuneration. The inspected person apologized to the affected clients after the doctor's misconduct was discovered. The inspection was conducted by inspector PhDr. Petr Krejčí. Recommendation: It is necessary to pay due attention to the transfer of documents containing personal data. In particular, if the documents contain special categories of personal data, it is necessary to set up sufficient control mechanisms to ensure that they are not made available to an unauthorized person through the error of a single person.
