

Path: Home page > Main menu > Supervisory and decision-making activity Control of the processing of personal data of premium payers (Sectoral health insurance company for employees of banks, insurance companies and the construction industry)



On the basis of two complaints, the Office carried out an inspection, the subject of which was the processing of personal data of insurance premium payers of Oborová zdravotno pojišťovna employees of banks, insurance companies and the construction industry who owe insurance premiums. The health insurance company in question published on its website the personal data of payers whose total debt for health insurance premiums and penalties exceeded CZK 10,000. Due to the fact that the inspected person immediately corrected the defective condition, the Office did not impose measures to eliminate the identified deficiencies and waived the imposition of a fine. The inspection was conducted by inspector PhDr. Petr Krejčí. Recommendation: The Office considers the publication of debtors' personal data to be a coercive act, which usually violates the basic rights and freedoms of data subjects. Any disclosure of personal data in connection with the creation of claims would only be possible with the prior consent of the data subject, subject to compliance with all basic principles of personal data processing, which would, however, have to meet all the requirements of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, i.e. it would have to be possible, for example, to revoke at any time.
