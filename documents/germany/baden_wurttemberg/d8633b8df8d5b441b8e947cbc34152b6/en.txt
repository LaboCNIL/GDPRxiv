PRESS RELEASE

 

 

 Podcast Freedom of Data, Episode 18:

In conversation with Constanze Kurz

 

A new episode of the LfDI podcast "Data Freedom" is on

available for download on the website of the state commissioner. In

of the current episode, LfDI Dr. Stefan Brink and Dr.

Constanze Kurz, spokeswoman for the Chaos Computer Club,

Publicist and expert on data protection and

Data security, what both have to do with each other, and

discuss about the danger of tampering with personal

data in the network.

 

Podcast data freedom: https://www.baden-

wuerttemberg.datenschutz.de/datenfreiheit/
