  First aid for doctors!

First aid FAQ for medical practices on the implementation of the GDPR

The State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (LfDI)

Answer today to the most urgent questions about the implementation of the basic data protection

order in medical practices.

 

Especially the everyday work in the doctor's office raises due to the regular handling

with the particularly sensitive health data of the patients

Questions on. In particular, there is a need for information among resident doctors

high. In the past, the existing uncertainty was already counteracted with training

of the LfDI, among other things, in the associations of statutory health insurance physicians and, of course, in

management practice of his company.

Nevertheless, questions arise again and again, especially in daily implementation;

in particular the question of the need for declarations of consent or

Information notices drive the practice. For these cases, the present question

ge answer catalog is a first aid for medical practices, but also orientation for

patients.

This is a first draft for a dynamic document that

be supplemented in the future by further questions from the consulting practice of the LfDI

the will. In this way, a comprehensive practical aid for the health care

arise in order to prevent undesirable developments in practice.
