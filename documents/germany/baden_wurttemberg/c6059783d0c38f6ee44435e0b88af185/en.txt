  First IFG Days Baden-Württemberg

 

June 3, 2019

Thursday, June 6, 2019: Cinema session with the state representative
 
As part of the first IFG-Days Baden-Württemberg, the state representative for
Data protection and freedom of information on Thursday, June 6, 2019 in cooperation with the
Stuttgart city center cinemas the exciting secret service thriller "The End of Truth".
The film starts at 8:00 p.m. at CINEMA, Königstr. 22 (2nd floor), Stuttgart.
 
A film by Philipp Leinemann with Axel Prahl, Claudia Michaelsen, Ronald Zehrfeld and
Alexander Fehling, the 2019 German Film Prize, in the category: Best Male
got a supporting role.
  
A film about intrigues, abuse of power and corruption.
  
To the movie:
Martin Behrens, an employee of the BND, passes on information that a drone attack
of the Americans. A few days later there is an attack in one
Munich restaurant, where Behrens' secret lover dies.
  
After the film, visitors can enjoy a view of the illuminated city silhouette
Discuss their impressions with the state representative over small snacks.
  
The cinema evening takes place as part of the first IFG-Days Baden-Württemberg.
  
You can find more information about our IFG-Days at:
https://www.baden-wuerttemberg.datenschutz.de/einladung-zu-den-ersten-ifg-days-in-bawue/
  
With the series of events "Data protection as a cultural task", the LfDI illuminates the
ma data protection from different perspectives. This unusual approach finds
its continuation now also in the area of the freedom of information.
  
The state representative is looking forward to an extraordinary evening with exciting and
stimulating conversations.
  
Admission of 7.80 euros includes admission to the cinema, nibbles after the
Film in the foyer and an incomparable view of the Schlossplatz.
  
Tickets are now available at the box office of the city center cinemas and at:
