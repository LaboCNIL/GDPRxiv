Berlin, October 6, 2022

 

 

House of Representatives elects new Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and

Freedom of Information

 
The Berlin House of Representatives today appointed Meike Kamp as the new Berlin Commissioner for

Data Protection and Freedom of Information (BlnBDI). The lawyer succeeds Maja

Smoltczyk, whose term of office ended at the end of October 2021.

 

Volker Brozio, Acting Head of the BlnBDI: "I welcome the election of Meike Kamp

to the new Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information. That means after a year

determined who will succeed Maja Smoltczyk. I'm happy to welcome Meike Kamp to our

to welcome the authorities and to hand over the official business to them in the near future.‚Äù
