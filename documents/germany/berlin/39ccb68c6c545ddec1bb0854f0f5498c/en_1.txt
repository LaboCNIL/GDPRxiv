Berlin data protection officer at the re:publica Netzfest

Also this year, the Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information
May 4th and 5th, 2019 with an information stand at the Netzfest of the re:publica 2019 in the Park am
Gleisdreieck (East Park) represented.
 
In the program track Politics & Society of the digital folk festival, three employees of the
authority before their work. In addition to the perennial General Data Protection Regulation,
This year, a special focus is dedicated to the topic of media education. While
Throughout the festival we will answer questions about data protection at the information stand.
