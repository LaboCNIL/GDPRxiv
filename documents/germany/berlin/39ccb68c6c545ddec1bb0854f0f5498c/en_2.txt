Sunday, May 5, 2019 from 11:30 a.m. – 12:15 p.m.:
Data protection supervision under GDPR: Who we are, how we work
and what the new law has brought
Lecture by Dr. Philipp Stroh, legal advisor to the BlnBDI
 
Citizens of Berlin see themselves at any time from a large number of
exposed to risks for the protection of their personal data; may be
it in the waiting room of a practice, when renting an apartment
or by looking at the e-mail inbox. The data protection supervisory
net of these dangers on a daily basis and represents the interests of those affected
individuals against such companies and authorities. Philipp straw presents
present how the work at the data protection authority is carried out, what obstacles
exist and how one can take action oneself.
