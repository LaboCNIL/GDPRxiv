
New press spokesman for the state commissioner for data protection in Lower Saxony



Since June 1, 2018, Johannes Pepping has been in charge of press and public relations at the Lower Saxony State Commissioner for Data Protection. The 38-year-old graduate journalist previously worked in the Federal Government's Press and Information Office in Berlin.
Johannes Pepping studied journalism and history in Dortmund. In addition, he completed a traineeship in private radio. As a journalist, he mainly worked for Westdeutscher Rundfunk as an online and television editor. Most recently, he was responsible for the federal government's press work in the chief of service department at the Federal Press Office.
