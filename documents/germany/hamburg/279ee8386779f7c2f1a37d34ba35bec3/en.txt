Under the motto "Data protection of the two speeds?" Prof. Dr. Johannes Caspar, the Hamburg Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information, presented his 28th Activity Report Data Protection 2019.


Thursday, February 13, 2020 at 10:00 a.m
Hamburg City Hall, Room 151


The current 28th activity report presents the focal points of the complex work of the Hamburg data protection authority in the 2019 reporting year. Once again, the report is based on an output-oriented focus. Divided into the five main chapters of audits, reports, legally binding orders/fine proceedings, advice/data protection communication and information on official activities, the most important cases and focal points from the 2019 reporting period are presented on around 180 pages. These include, for example, the following topics: Interim report on the GDPR and prospects for the evaluation in the EU, fine proceedings of the HmbBfDI, VIDEMO, the amendment of the Constitutional Protection Act or tracking.
The Hamburg Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information Prof. Dr. In addition to the above-mentioned topics, Caspar will also report on important, current data protection cases in the area of responsibility at the press conference and will be available for questions.
