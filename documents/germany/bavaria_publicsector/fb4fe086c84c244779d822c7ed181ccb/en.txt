Exemption from the mask requirement
for health reasons
The Bavarian State Commissioner for Data Protection
and the Bavarian State Office for Data Protection Supervision
publish new joint paper

Munich and Ansbach, June 28, 2021

According to the currently applicable 13th Bavarian Infection Protection Measures, the mask requirement
regulation continues to be a key tool in combating the
COVID-19 Pandemic. However, some affected persons can – in particular from
health reasons - do not wear a mask. The Bavarian legislator has the
Regulations for exemption from the mask requirement have now been revised. When applying this
Regulations also raise data protection issues.

The Bavarian State Commissioner for Data Protection and the Bavarian State Office for
Data protection supervisors now have their findings on this topic in a common
Current short information bundled. In it you express yourself, for example, on the design of liberation
certificates, the authorization to inspect such certificates, as well as the documentation
speaking controls.

The new joint paper is available at https://www.datenschutz-bayern.de in the “Da-
tenschutzreform 2018 – current brief information” and at https://www.lda.bayern.de
available.
