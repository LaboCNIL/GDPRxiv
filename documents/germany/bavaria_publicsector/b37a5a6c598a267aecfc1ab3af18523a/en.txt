Homepage of the data protection conference goes online

https://www.datenschutzkonferenz-online.de

Press Release – Page 1/1
Munich, July 20, 2018

The conference of independent data protection authorities of the federal and
of the countries (data protection conference) goes with a joint home
page online. Effective immediately, the new information platform
their resolutions, guidance and briefs of the data
protection conference available. About new contents of this homepage
Interested parties can be informed via an RSS feed.

The homepage of the data protection conference is at
https://datenschutzkonferenz-online.de.

Petri: “The joint homepage of the data protection conference bundles
a lot of information of transnational importance for the
public as well as for the non-public area. Bavarian public
Offices can still find all the information relevant to them on the
tenschutz on the website of the Bavarian State Commissioner for the
Privacy."

Prof. Dr. Thomas Petri

The data protection conference consists of the independent federal data protection authorities
and the countries.
Its aim is to uphold and protect fundamental data protection rights, to
to achieve the application of European and national data protection law and work together for
to enter into its further development.

The Bavarian State Commissioner for Data Protection checks the Bavarian public
public bodies compliance with data protection regulations. It's from Bavarian
Elected state parliament, independent and not bound by instructions from anyone
