Relocation of the State Commissioner for Data Protection

 

New address from March 1, 2023

 

The State Commissioner for Data Protection will move into other premises on March 1, 2023

in the city center of Magdeburg. The new address is:

 

 

State representative for data protection

Otto-von-Guericke-Strasse 34a

39104 Magdeburg

The telephone and fax numbers, the e-mail addresses and the PO box address remain the same

unchanged.

 

The State Commissioner for Data Protection points out that all of the processing

bodies responsible for personal data in the public and non-public

area, in whose data protection declarations the full address of the state representative

is specified, these must be adjusted.
