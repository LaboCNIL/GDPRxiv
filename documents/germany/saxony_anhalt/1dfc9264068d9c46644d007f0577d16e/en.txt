End of Term / Representative in Office

 

Representative in Office Albert Cohaus exercises powers pending election

State representative for data protection

 

 

The term of office of the previous state representative ended on December 31, 2020

carried out for data protection Dr. Harold von Bose. The choice of a

new state commissioner for data protection by the state parliament of Saxony-An-

halt has not yet taken place.

 

Until a successor is appointed, the State Commissioner

was responsible for data protection in accordance with Section 22 (3) of the General Data Protection Regulation

filling law of Saxony-Anhalt by the director of the office, Mr. Albert

Cohaus, represented. For the duration of the representation, the director of the office

holds the powers of the state commissioner for data protection.

 

As representative in office, Mr. Albert Cohaus also takes on the task of

of the commissioner for the freedom of information according to ยง 12 paragraph 2 of the information

transition law of Saxony-Anhalt. In this function he will lead the 2021

chair of the Conference of Information Free-
