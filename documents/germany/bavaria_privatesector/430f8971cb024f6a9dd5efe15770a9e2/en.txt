Information on data protection

 

The DS-GVO requires that the persons concerned be comprehensively informed about the data processing

be mized. This includes information on the legal basis, recipients of the data, rights

of the persons concerned, such as deletion, information, objection and the right to lodge a complaint

at a supervisory authority.

 

Possibility of objection by those affected

 

Apple has explained to us that every citizen has the opportunity to contact Apple

and to demand pixelation of their own house fronts. information, which contact

things Apple offers and when which cities or regions the Apple vehicles travel to

can be found on the BayLDA website at www.lda.bayern.de/apple-kameratouren.

 

Supervisory authority

 

As the European head office is based in Ireland, the Irish regulator is

responsible for data processing by Apple in Europe. The BayLDA accepts all

complaints from Germany and forwards them to the Irish supervisory authority.
