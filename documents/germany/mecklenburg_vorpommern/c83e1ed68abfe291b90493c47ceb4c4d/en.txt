The Media Guides MV project was launched on the initiative of the State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania
Parents in particular face a constantly changing challenge. Children and young people move about in digital worlds earlier and more naturally: new apps, new games, new technologies, new social networks. Media use in the family can quickly become a topic of dispute.
