With the adopted position paper "Facilitating access to information in the authorities through 'freedom of information by design'", the conference calls on the legislator to create the legal basis and framework conditions so that public administration can incorporate the requirements for freedom of information from the very beginning in the design of their IT systems and organizational processes: "Freedom of information by design".
