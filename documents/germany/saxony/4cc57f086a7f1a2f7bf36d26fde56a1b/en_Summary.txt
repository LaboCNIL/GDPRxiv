

The Saxon Data Protection Officer answers frequently asked questions in connection with facility-related vaccination requirements.



According to the Infection Protection Act, people who work in hospitals, medical practices or outpatient care services, for example, must be either vaccinated or recovered from March 15, 2022. With the so-called institution-related vaccination obligation in the health sector, there are numerous data protection issues, for example which data may be processed, what content a medical certificate must have or whether copies of the evidence submitted may be made.
