

The conference of the independent federal and state data protection supervisory authorities invites you to the 16th European Data Protection Day.



This year's topic is "The digital wallet in the EU - data protection nightmare or milestone for shaping Europe's digital future?" The event will take place on January 28, 2022 at 10 a.m. via live stream. The link to the stream and information on the process can be found on the website of the Independent Data Protection Center Saarland.
