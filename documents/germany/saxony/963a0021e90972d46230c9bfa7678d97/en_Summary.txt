Back to school in Saxony: new information on data protection for parents, teachers and students


 
Press

 
e-mail


09/03/2021

Around 494,500 schoolchildren will start a new school year next Monday. Also present: around 39,900 first graders. Their everyday life is now changing and this is associated with numerous questions that parents are now asking: How can my child use a tablet without their data falling into the wrong hands? Can the school make a copy of a mask exemption certificate? Can proof of vaccination, for example measles vaccination, be requested from my child? What should be considered when filming and photographing school events?
