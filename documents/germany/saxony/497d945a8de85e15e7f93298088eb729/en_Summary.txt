06/17/2021
The Saxon Data Protection Officer Andreas Schurig today presented his activity report for the past year to Andrea Dombois, First Vice-President of the Saxon State Parliament.
The focal points of the supervisory activity, statistics, information on the interpretation of the General Data Protection Regulation, sanctions practice and data protection case law are summarized on almost 180 pages.
