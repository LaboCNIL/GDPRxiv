More protection against cyber attacks is needed


 
Press

 
e-mail


05.11.2021
In view of the recent hacker attacks against German companies, the Saxon data protection officer Andreas Schurig advocates significantly more prevention. Each cyber attack costs more than 21,000 euros on average. Therefore, Saxony's companies should not wait until they have become the victim of hacker attacks. On the contrary: Prevention is more effective and more important than ever before to ensure that customer and employee data does not fall into the wrong hands.
