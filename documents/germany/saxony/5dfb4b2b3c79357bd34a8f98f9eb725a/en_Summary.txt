Press release on the presentation of the activity report of the Saxon data protection officer for the year 2019


 
To press

 
e-mail


12/22/2020
On December 22, 2020, the Saxon Data Protection Officer Andreas Schurig will present the activity report for the past year. The report covers the period from January 1, 2019 to December 31, 2019. Due to the pandemic, there will be no press conference this year, only a written press release. We ask for your understanding for this.
