

40th Anniversary of the Council of Europe Convention 108 on Data Protection



On the occasion of European Data Protection Day and to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Council of Europe's Data Protection Convention 108, representatives from politics, the judiciary and administration will come together via video transmission on January 28, 2021 to exchange views on the challenges of international data transfer.
The preparation of the online event was the responsibility of the Federal Ministry of the Interior, Building and Community as well as the Saxon data protection officer as representative of the conference of the independent data protection supervisory authorities of the federal and state governments.
