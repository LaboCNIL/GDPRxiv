                                    08/28/2019
                                LfDI publishes third activity report on freedom of informationThe State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information of Rhineland-Palatinate (LfDI) publishes its third activity report on freedom of information. The reporting period (from January 1, 2016 to December 31, 2017) was characterized in particular by the entry into force of the State Transparency Act of Rhineland-Palatinate on January 1, 2016. The activity report summarizes the main changes due to the new legal situation and provides information on the focal points of the work of the LfDI in the area of freedom of information and describes selected results from his consulting work. It also shows the development of freedom of information law in the reporting period. The third activity report on freedom of information and the other activity reports on freedom of information and data protection are available here.
