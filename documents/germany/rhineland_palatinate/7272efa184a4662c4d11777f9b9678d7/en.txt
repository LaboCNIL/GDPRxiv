                                Virtual visit of the Transparency Law elective subject groupOn December 3, 2021, the students of the Transparency Law elective course at the Rhineland-Palatinate University of Public Administration paid a virtual visit to the Rhineland-Palatinate State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (LfDI). Employees of the state representative explained to the students the role and tasks of the state representative in the area of freedom of information. The participants then discussed cases from the advisory practice of the LfDI and examined the scope of the right to information access and its limits based on the specifications of the state transparency law.
