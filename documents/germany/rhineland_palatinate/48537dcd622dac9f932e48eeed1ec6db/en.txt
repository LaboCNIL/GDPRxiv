                                    01/10/2019
                                Expansion of the Internet offering in the area of freedom of information at the start of the year The State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information of Rhineland-Palatinate (LfDI) further expanded its Internet offering in the area of freedom of information at the start of the year. Since the beginning of 2019, citizens and bodies subject to transparency obligations have been able to find a great deal of information on freedom of information and the State Transparency Act of Rhineland-Palatinate on the website. Among other things, the range of information has been expanded to include the most frequently asked questions (FAQs) on the State Transparency Act. You can find FAQs for citizens as well as FAQs for bodies subject to transparency obligations. The questions asked from the point of view of the citizens should help them to get the information they want from the administration at the lowest possible cost and with little effort. The FAQs for bodies subject to transparency help them to fulfill their obligations under the State Transparency Act through a procedure that offers transparency and is at the same time appropriate
