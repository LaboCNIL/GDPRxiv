                                "Privacy goes cinema" will show "THE CLEANERS" on October 8, 2018 at CineMayence in MainzThe State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information of Rhineland-Palatinate (LfDI), Prof. Dr. Dieter Kugelmann, has approved the continuation of his series "Privacy goes cinema". Documentary film "THE CLEANERS" was selected. The film will be shown on October 8th at 8.30 p.m. at the CineMayence in Mainz and afterwards the viewers will be able to discuss with the state commissioner again.THE CLEANERS reveals a gigantic shadow industry of digital censorship in Manila, where in the On behalf of the Silicon Valley corporations, incriminating photos and videos are outsourced to the "content moderators" from Facebook, YouTube, Twitter & CO.
