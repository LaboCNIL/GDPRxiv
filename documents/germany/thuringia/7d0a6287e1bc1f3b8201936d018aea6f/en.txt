Erfurt, September 16, 2019

The Thuringian Transparency Act was passed on September 11, 2019 by the Thuringian

Parliament decided! What changes?

On the occasion of the International Freedom of Information Day, the Thuringian

State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (TLfDI), Dr. Lutz

Hasse, interested journalists to an information event on

Topic: “Transparency and Freedom of Information Laws – Valuable Tools

for journalists in their information research?”. It will be them

Opportunities, but also the limits of information procurement and their

practicability discussed. dr Jost Müller-Neuhof, legal policy correspondent

at the Tagesspiegel, lawyer and lecturer for legal communication am

Faculty of Law at the Freie Universität Berlin and member of the

German Press Council, will be a guest speaker on the subject and his experiences

report and discuss with you and the TLfDI.

When: October 2, 2019, 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m
