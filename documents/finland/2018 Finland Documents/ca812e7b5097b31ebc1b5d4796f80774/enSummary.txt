 The telephone service number of the Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman will change on 4 June 2018. The telephone service provides general advice and guidance relating to data protection and will inform you if your case requires further investigation and processing by the Office. Unfortunately, the telephone service is very busy. Answers to frequently asked questions are available on our website.
