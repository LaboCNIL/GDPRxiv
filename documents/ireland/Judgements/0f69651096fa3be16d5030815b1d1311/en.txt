Record No.
2020/305 JR
Type of action and Venue
Judicial Review, High Court
Date of Judgment Order
29 June 2020 (Final Order)
Outcome
On 1 May 2020, the Applicants applied to the High Court by way of judicial review to seek certain orders in relation to the conduct on an ongoing statutory inquiry being undertaken by the DPC into the processing of location data by Google.  Certain terms having been agreed between the parties, the proceedings were struck out, on consent, on 29 June 2020. No order for costs was made.
Current Status of Case 
Proceedings discontinued.
