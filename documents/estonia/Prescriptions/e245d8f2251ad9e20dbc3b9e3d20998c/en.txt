Tatari tn 39/10134 Tallinn / 627 4135 / info@aki.ee / www.aki.ee Registry code 70004235 REVOCATION OF A PRECAUTION WARNING in personal data protection matter no. 2.1.-6/20/23 Date of compilation: 10.06.2020, Tallinn Position and name of the compiler: Raiko Kaur, Senior Inspector of the Data Protection Inspectorate Addressee: BB Trade Estonia OÜ contact@thompsonstein.com gdpr@bitbay.net Circumstances: The Data Protection Inspectorate made 09.06.2020 mandatory precept-warning No. 2.1.-6/20/23 pursuant to § 56 (1), (2) (8), § 58 (1) and Article 58 (1) (a) and (e) of the General Data Protection Regulation, by which BB Trade Estonia OÜ to respond to the inquiry sent to the Data Protection Inspectorate No. 2.1.-1/20/724 on 19.05.2020. After issuing the precept-warning, BB Trade Estonia OÜ sent a letter to the e-mail address raiko.kaur@aki.ee, which showed that the company had responded to the inspection's inquiry on 02.06.2020. Based on the fact that BB Trade Estonia OÜ had replied to the Inspectorate before the precept was issued and the reply had not reached the person due to a technical error of the Inspectorate, there was no need to issue a precept. Decision: Based on the above and guided by § 65 (1) of the Administrative Procedure Act, the Data Protection Inspectorate shall revoke the precept-warning No. 2.1.-6/20/23 as of 09.06.2020. / digitally signed / Raiko Kaur Senior Inspector on behalf of the Director General
