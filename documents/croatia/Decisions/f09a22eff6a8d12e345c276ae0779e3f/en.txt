
If you receive an e-mail from an e-mail address that looks like it was sent from the official e-mail addresses of the Personal Data Protection Agency (addresses that have azop.hr after the @ sign - e.g. zop@azop.hr, admin@azop. hr) with the title "Third parties have access to your account", please delete it immediately, do not respond to it or forward it!
Read more about phishing at the link: https://azop.hr/phishing-napadi-kako-ih-prepoznati-i-zastititi-se/
