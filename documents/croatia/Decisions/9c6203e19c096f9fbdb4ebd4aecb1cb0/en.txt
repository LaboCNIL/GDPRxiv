
The Spring Conference is the permanent and largest conference of European Commissioners for Personal Data Protection, held at least once a year and exchanging experiences on topics in the field of personal data protection, reaching conclusions and proposals for relevant EU bodies, the Council of Europe and others.
The host of the jubilee 30th Spring Conference of European Data Protection Supervisors in 2021 is the Personal Data Protection Agency. It will be held in Cavtat in the period from 13 to 15 May 2021.
Also, the Personal Data Protection Agency is a member of the Subgroup for the Future of the Conference, which aims to actively contribute to improving the field of personal data protection in Europe and beyond and achieving the strategic goals of the Spring Conference.
