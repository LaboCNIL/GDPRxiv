
The Agency for Personal Data Protection in cooperation with the Croatian Chamber of Commerce invites you to a free online workshop "Protection of personal data and harmonization with the GDPR in the security industry", which will be held on Friday, November 20, 2020.
You can apply by filling out the application form at the link https://www.hgk.hr/besplatna-online-radionica-zastita-osobnih-podataka-i-uskladivanje-s-opcom-uredbom-gdpr-om-u-zastitarskoj-djelatnosti- 20112020-announcement
The workshop is intended exclusively for security managers and executors, so the program is tailored to the specific needs and concerns they face in their daily work regarding compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation and the General Implementation Act.
The participants of the workshop will have the opportunity to get acquainted with the legal framework in the field of personal data protection, and the focus of the workshop is on specifics in the field of personal data protection that directly affect the application of the Private Protection Act. At the same time, through specific examples, certain ambiguities related to the protection of personal data that appear in practice will be clarified, ie. the conduct of small and medium-sized enterprises performing private security activities.
This workshop is one of the activities of the Personal Data Protection Agency within the implementation of the EU project ARC (Awareness Raising Campaign for SMEs), with the aim of supporting micro, small and medium enterprises in aligning business processes with the provisions of the General Data Protection Regulation. GDPR).
