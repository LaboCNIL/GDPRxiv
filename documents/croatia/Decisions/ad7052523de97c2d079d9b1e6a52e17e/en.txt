
The Director of the Personal Data Protection Agency Zdravko Vukić was a guest on the show Consumer Code on the occasion of the 3rd anniversary of the full implementation of the General Regulation on Data Protection. He spoke about the role of the Agency in the personal data protection system in Croatia, the most common complaints of citizens, imposed and future penalties, emphasizing the importance of raising public awareness of the importance of personal data protection, which 3 years after full implementation of not at a satisfactory level.
