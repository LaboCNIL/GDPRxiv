The Personal Data Protection Agency, in cooperation with the Croatian Association of Employers, is organizing the workshop "How to harmonize business with the General Data Protection Regulation during the pandemic?". The workshop will be held at Radnička 37a/0, 10 000, Zagreb, on December 16, 2021, from 12:30 p.m. to 2:30 p.m.
The workshop was organized within the framework of the ARC project - Awareness raising campaign for SMEs - a campaign to raise awareness about data protection among micro, small and medium-sized enterprises, which is co-financed by the EU Program on Equality and Citizenship Rights https://arc-rec-project.eu/ title/.
