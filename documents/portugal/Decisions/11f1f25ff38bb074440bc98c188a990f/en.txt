0 NATIONAL COMMISSION C ON DATA PROTECTION
OPINION/2020/17
Banco de Portugal asked the National Data Protection Commission (CNPD) to comment on the draft Instruction that defines the forms and terms for sending the report on the activity carried out, in national territory, by financial entities based in another state. member of the European Union operating in Portugal under the regime of freedom to provide services.
The request made and the present opinion fall within the attributions and powers of the CNPD, as the national authority for controlling the processing of personal data, in accordance with the provisions of subparagraph c) of paragraph 1 of article 57 and paragraph 1 of article 57. 4 of article 36 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 (General Regulation on Data Protection - RGPD), in conjunction with the provisions of article 3. , in Article 4(2) and Article 6(1)(a), all of Law No. 58/2019, of August 8 (which aims to ensure the execution , in the domestic legal order, of the GDPR).
Banco de Portugal's draft instruction does not raise questions from the point of view of personal data protection.
Approved at the plenary meeting of February 4, 2020
