I NATIONAL COMMISSION t ON DATA PROTECTION
RESOLUTION/2020/170
Considering the declaration of a state of alert and the measures adopted by the Portuguese Government;
Considering the guidelines of public health authorities to reduce the risk of contamination by CoVid-19, in light of the principles of prevention and precaution;
And without prejudice to the provisions of article 14 of Decree-Law No. 10-A/2020, of 13 March;
The National Data Protection Commission determines that the deadlines for responding to deliberation projects are interrupted until the declaration, by the competent sovereign body, of the end of the exceptional period that the country is going through because of the pandemic.
The deadlines set in the projects begin to be counted, again, on the business day following the official publication of such declaration.
The interruption of deadlines applies immediately.
Lisbon, March 16, 2020
Filipa Calvão (President)
