EUROPEAN DATA PROTECTION DAY


European Data Protection Day marks the 38th anniversary of the signing in Strasbourg on 28 January 1981 of Convention 108 for the Protection of Individuals with regard to Automatic Processing of Personal Data, which marked the beginning of the European regulation of data protection. rules for the processing of personal data.
We are currently in a new stage of development in the field of data protection following the application, from 25 May 2018, of Regulation 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data. personal data and the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC.
To celebrate the European Day of Data Protection, the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing will organize the Conference on "Ensuring compliance with the European Data Protection Regulation and applicable national regulations" at the Palace of Parliament on January 28, 2019.
The event aims to provide an opportunity for real debates on the application of the new requirements of the General Regulation on data protection, of Law no. 129/2018 and of Law no. 190/2018 on some measures for the implementation of the General Data Protection Regulation, also related to the competences of the Authority.
The informative materials dedicated to the European Data Protection Day are available at www.dataprotection.ro/information general / information of public interest / informative materials
