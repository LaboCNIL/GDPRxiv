16.04.2021
EDPB Plenary - April

At the Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board, held online on 13 April 2021, the following were mainly adopted:

 Opinion no. 14/2021 on the draft Decision of the European Commission on the recognition of an adequate level of protection in the United Kingdom, pursuant to the General Data Protection Regulation
 Opinion no. 15/2021 on the draft European Commission Decision recognizing an adequate level of protection in the United Kingdom under Directive 680/2018
 Guide no. 8/2020 on highlighting users in social media (final form)
 Guide no. 3/2021 regarding the application of art. 65 para. (1) lit. a) of the General Regulation on Data Protection - in public consultation for 6 weeks
