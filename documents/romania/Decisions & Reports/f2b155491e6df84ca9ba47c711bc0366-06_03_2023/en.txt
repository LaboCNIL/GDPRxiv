During the online Plenary on February 28, 2023, the European Committee for Data Protection adopted Opinion no. 5/2023 regarding the draft European Commission Decision regarding an adequate level of protection according to the US and EU General Framework in the field of data protection.
In this context, we note that, on 13.12.2022, the European Commission published the draft Decision on the New US-EU General Framework, based on which the transfers of personal data will be carried out (which will replace the previous agreement known as the Privacy Shield , invalidated by the EU Court of Justice in the 2020 Schrems II Case).
More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news
