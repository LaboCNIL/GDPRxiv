NEW FINES IN THE APPLICATION OF THE RGPD

1. The National Supervisory Authority, in the exercise of its investigative powers, sanctioned the operator Modern Barber S.R.L, on 26.11.2019 with a fine in the amount of 14329.5 lei, the equivalent of the amount of 3000 EURO.
The sanction was applied to the operator Modern Barber S.R.L for the deed provided by art. 83 para. (5) of Regulation (EU) 679/2016, reported to art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) and corroborated with art. 8 of O.G. no. 2/2001.
Thus, Modern Barber S.R.L did not provide the information requested by the National Supervisory Authority, thus violating the provisions of art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) of Regulation (EU) no. 679/2016.
Therefore, the sanction of the warning was applied to the operator and the corrective measure was ordered consisting in obliging the operator to transmit to the Authority, in writing, all the requested information, within 10 calendar days from the communication of the minutes.
Subsequently, it was found that the operator Modern Barber S.R.L did not comply with the corrective measure ordered and the measure to amend it was taken.
At the same time, the corrective measure was ordered to transmit to the Authority, in writing, all the requested information, within 5 calendar days from the communication of the minutes.

2. The National Supervisory Authority, in the exercise of its investigative powers, sanctioned on 02.12.2019 the operator Nicola Medical Team 17 SRL with a fine in the amount of 9555.4 lei, the equivalent of the amount of 2000 EURO, for the deed provided by art. 83 para. (5) and (6) of Regulation (EU) 679/2016, referred to in art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) and corroborated with art. 8 of O.G. no. 2/2001.
Thus, Nicola Medical Team 17 SRL did not provide the information requested by the National Supervisory Authority, violating the provisions of art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) of Regulation (EU) no. 679/2016.
The warning sanction was applied and the corrective measure was ordered consisting in obliging the operator to transmit to the Authority, in writing, all the requested information within 10 calendar days from the communication of the minutes.
As the controller did not comply with the corrective action ordered, it was amended and, at the same time, the corrective action was ordered to send in writing to the National Supervisory Authority all the information previously requested regarding the conformity of the processing operations performed as operator. data, within 5 days from the communication of the minutes.
