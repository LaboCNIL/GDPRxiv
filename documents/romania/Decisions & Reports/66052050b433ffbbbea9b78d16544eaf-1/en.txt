26.11.2021
EDPB plenary in November

During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, which took place on November 19, 2021, Guideline no. 5/2021 regarding the correlation between the application of art. 3 and the provisions relating to international transfers in Chapter V of the General Data Protection Regulation.
This document will be in public consultation until the end of January 2022, during which time proposals can be sent from those interested.
More information is available at:
