Press release

The National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing issued Decision no. 161 of October 9, 2018 regarding the approval of the Investigation Procedure. It was published in the Official Gazette of Romania no. 892 of October 23, 2018, Part I and entered into force on the date of publication. This Decision approves the Investigation Procedure set out in the Annex.
This decision, published on the Authority's website under the section "Legislation / ANSPDCP / Published Decisions", can also be consulted here. "
