Address of the EDPS on the draft Guide on applications to support the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic

The 21st Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board took place on 14 April 2020.
At its Plenary Session on 14 April 2020, the European Data Protection Board adopted an address on the European Commission's draft Guide on applications to support the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic.
The address was adopted following a request for consultation from the European Commission.
The address of the European Data Protection Board is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/letters/edpb-letter-concerning-european-commissions-draft-guidance-apps_en.
The European Commission guide is available at: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?qid=1587141168991&uri=CELEX%3A52020XC0417%2808%29.
