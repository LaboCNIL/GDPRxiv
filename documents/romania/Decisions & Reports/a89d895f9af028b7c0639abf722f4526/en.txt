OPEN DOORS DAY - January 31, 2020

On January 31, 2020, on the occasion of the celebration of the European Data Protection Day, the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing organizes the "Open Day".
On this occasion, during Friday, between 10:00 and 14:00, we invite people interested in the field of personal data protection to the headquarters of our institution in B-dul Magheru no. 28-30, et. 5, cam. 3.
You will have the opportunity to visit the areas open to the public on this occasion and to receive general information on the application of the New General Regulation on Data Protection and incidental national regulations, as well as on the specific activity of the National Supervisory Authority.
