EDPS Declaration on the Processing of Personal Data in the Context of the COVID-19 Pandemic

On 19 March 2020, the European Data Protection Board adopted the Declaration on the Processing of Personal Data in the Context of the COVID-19 Pandemic.
The statement by the Chair of the European Data Protection Board in the context of the Coronavirus pandemic is available here.
The English version is available at the following link: https://edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/other/statement-processing-personal-data-context-covid-19-outbreak_en.
