19.03.2021
EDPB plenary

During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, held online on March 9, 2021, the following documents were mainly adopted:

 Guide no. 9/2020 regarding relevant and reasoned objection (final form after public consultation)
 Guide no. 1/2020 on connected vehicles (final form after public consultation)
 Guide no. 2/2021 regarding virtual voice assistance (form for public consultation until 23.04.2021)
 Joint opinion with EDPS no. 3/2021 regarding the proposal for a Regulation of the European Parliament and the Council on data governance
 Statement on the draft ePrivacy Regulation.

More information is available at:
