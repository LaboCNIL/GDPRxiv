conference
on the application of the General Data Protection Regulation
(in May 2019)

On May 21, 2019, the representatives of the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing participated in the event “GDPR Talks 2019 - Balance after one year of GDPR”, organized by Concord Communication.
In the first section of the event, the stage of application of Regulation (EU) 679/2016 in Romania in 2019 was discussed, including at the level of public institutions.
On this occasion, the representatives of the National Supervisory Authority presented a balance of their own activity after one year of application of the new data protection rules.
Participants were also provided with statistical data on the work of the Authority, namely the number of data protection officers, security breaches of notified data, investigations carried out and sanctions imposed.
The second panel of the event was dedicated to the discussions regarding the application of the provisions of Law no. Regulation (EC) No 190/2018 on measures for the implementation of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing of Directive 95/46 / EC (General Data Protection Regulation).
On this occasion, issues related to the application of the RGPD in the context of labor relations were analyzed, as well as issues related to the appointment of a data protection officer, data security and notification of security breaches.
On May 23, 2019, the representatives of the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing participated in the Conference on “Data Protection; Solutions and responsibilities - one year after the entry into force of the GDPR ”, organized by the Bucharest Chamber of Commerce and Industry and Universul Juridic Publishing House.
The event carried out an analysis of the level of compliance one year after the entry into force of the General Data Protection Regulation and supported companies and public authorities, clarifying a number of aspects of the Regulation such as: the main operators concerned; dilemmas in the interpretation of the Regulation; evaluation of contracts, policies, procedures and notifications related to the processing of personal data in order to establish compliance with the provisions of the Regulation; the role and effectiveness of a data protection officer; steps to follow and recommendations, effective solutions to comply with the Regulation.
On this occasion, the representatives of the National Supervisory Authority brought to the attention of the participants, within the specific panel, important aspects regarding the Procedure for conducting investigations carried out by the Authority, respectively: the legal framework; type of investigations; how to conduct investigations; the duties of control staff in investigations; the main obligations of the controlled operator; sanctions and corrective measures; appeals against the minutes of finding / sanctioning and the decisions issued by the Authority as well as a statistic of the control activity during the period May 25, 2018 - May 15, 2019.
The events, also reflected in the media, were marked by interactive discussions, which demonstrated the interest of the participants to comply with the new rules of personal data processing.
