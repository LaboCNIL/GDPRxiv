02.10.2020
Statistical data regarding the control activity of ANSPDCP

Between January 2020 and September 2020, the National Supervisory Authority received a number of 3952 complaints, 176 notifications and 128 notifications regarding security incidents, on the basis of which investigations were opened.
As a result of the investigations carried out, during this period 22 fines were applied in a total amount of 68,900 euros and 10,000 lei, respectively, a fine applied under Law no. 506/2004.
A further 46 warnings were issued and 42 corrective actions were taken.
Regarding the findings during the investigations, on this occasion the data operators were charged with violating the provisions of art. 5, art.6, art.7, art.9, art.12, art.14, art.15, art.25 and art.32 of the RGPD, as well as the violation of the provisions of art. 13 of Law no. 506/2004.
Corrective measures were also taken during the investigations, such as:

 compliance with the requests of data subjects by whom they have exercised their rights under the RGPD;
 ensuring the compliance of processing operations with the provisions of the RGPD;
 reviewing and updating the technical and organizational measures implemented, including the working procedures regarding the protection of personal data, as well as the implementation of measures regarding the regular training of persons acting under the authority of the operator, regarding the obligations under the RGPD, including regarding the risks involved in the processing of personal data, depending on the specifics of the activity;
 conducting a risk assessment for the rights and freedoms of individuals, including the classification of a degree of risk, taking into account the nature, scope, context and purposes of the processing;
 reviewing and updating the technical and organizational measures implemented as a result of the risk assessment for the rights and freedoms of individuals, including working procedures related to the protection of personal data.
