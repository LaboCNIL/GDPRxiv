Plenary of the European Data Protection Board

On February 12, 2019, the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection took place, a body with legal personality of the European Union, established under art. 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
On this occasion, the European Data Protection Board adopted the Guide on coding codes, intended to ensure a practical application of the provisions of art. 40-41 of the General Data Protection Regulation. It is in public consultation.
Opinion no. 4/2019 on administrative agreements, regarding transfers between EEA financial supervisors and those from third countries, pursuant to art. 46 para. (3) lit. b) of the General Regulation on Data Protection.
Information on the transfer of personal data in the context of Brexit was also adopted during this meeting, in the event of a UK leaving the European Union without an agreement after 30 March 2019, when the rules applicable to third countries, provided by art. . 46-49 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
At the same time, the Committee's work program for 2019-2020 was established.
