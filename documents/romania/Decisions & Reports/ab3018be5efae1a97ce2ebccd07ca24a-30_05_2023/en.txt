EDPB plenary session - May 2023

During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection held on May 24-25, 2023, the following main documents were adopted:

 Guide no. 4/2022 regarding the calculation of administrative fines, final form (after public consultation);
 Guide no. 3/2021 regarding the application of art. 65 para. (1) lit. a) from GDPR, final form (after public consultation).

On this occasion, elections were held for the positions of president and vice-president of the European Data Protection Board.
More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news
