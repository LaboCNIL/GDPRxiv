31.01.2023
Conference

At the online conference entitled "The effectiveness of GDPR application in a connected world", organized, on January 27, 2023, by the National Supervisory Authority for the Processing of Personal Data, to celebrate the European Day of Data Protection, representatives took part important representatives of some ministries, central public institutions, as well as numerous representatives of the most important professional associations and unions, together with those of some private companies from various sectors of activity.
The large participation testified to the special interest in applying the principles and rules of personal data protection to high standards.
The main topics presented referred to:

 processing minors' data
 international data transfers
 the implications of using new technologies
 the EDPB report on cookies
 the conditions of the operators' liability
 data processing in the context of tax investigations.

At the end of the conference, the President of the Authority thanked the guests for their involvement and expressed the openness of our institution for the continuation of collaboration with the public and private environment, in order to ensure effective compliance with the rules for the use of personal data.
