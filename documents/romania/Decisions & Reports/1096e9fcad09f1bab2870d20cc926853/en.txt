Working meetings with FIC and AmCham representatives

On December 18, 2019, a meeting was held at the headquarters of the Supervisory Authority with the representatives of the Council of Foreign Investors (FIC), during which various issues were discussed regarding the application of the General Data Protection Regulation (RGPD) in the context of infringement. data security, ensuring the transparency of the operator in relation to the data processing activities, as well as the framing of some processing on the basis of consent and / or legitimate interest.

Also, on December 19, 2019, the representatives of the Supervisory Authority participated in a meeting with the representatives of AmCham Romania, during which some aspects were discussed, mainly related to: RGPD requirements regarding the elaboration of codes of conduct, assessment of security incidents, establishing the quality of operator, proxy and associated operators, informing the persons concerned, processing special categories of data, monitoring / video surveillance of employees.
