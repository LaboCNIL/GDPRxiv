February 2020 plenary of the EDPB

The Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board took place on 18-19 February 2020, during which the following documents were mainly adopted:

 The Committee's contribution to the evaluation of the application of the European Data Protection Regulation, referred to in Art. 97
 The guide on data transfer between public authorities under the conditions of art. 46 para. (2) lit. a) and art. 46 para. (3) lit. b) of the European Regulation on Data Protection

The guide is subject to public consultation.
More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news/news_en
