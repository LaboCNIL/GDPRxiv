COMPLETING THE ONLINE DATA PROTECTION OFFICER FORM

We draw the attention of all operators and persons empowered by operators to the need to fill in the ON-LINE form for the declaration of the data protection officer existing on the authority's website under the section "Data protection officer".
In this sense, we inform you that according to the provisions of art. 37 para. (7) of EU Regulation 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC, the controller or the person authorized by the controller shall notify the supervisory authority the contact details of the data protection officer.
