During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, held online on May 4, 2022, the Joint Opinion with the EDPS was adopted regarding the proposal for a Regulation of the European Parliament and the Council on harmonized rules for fair access to data and fair use of them (Data Act - Data Law).
This document made several recommendations and proposed safeguards, including limitations on the use of data for direct marketing, employee monitoring or credit purposes.
During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, held online on May 12, 2022, mainly, the following were adopted:

 Guide no. 4/2022 on the calculation of fines under GDPR, which is subject to public debate until June 27, 2022
 Guide no. 5/2022 on facial recognition in the field of law enforcement bodies, under public debate until June 27, 2022
