Amendment of Law 102/2005

Starting with June 24, 2018, Law 129/2018 amending and supplementing Law no. 102/2005 regarding the establishment, organization and functioning of the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing, as well as for the abrogation of Law no. 677/2001 for the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, published in the Official Gazette of Romania no. 503 of 19.06.2018
This law is intended, mainly, to ensure the concordance between the national regulation and the monitoring and control competencies of the national supervisory authorities, established by the provisions of art. 55-59 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC (General Data Protection Regulation).
It has also been established that the National Authority for the Supervision of the Processing of Personal Data has the task of monitoring and controlling compliance with national legislation transposing Directive (EU) 2016/680 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of individuals. on the processing of personal data by the competent authorities for the purpose of the prevention, detection, investigation or prosecution of criminal offenses or the execution of sentences and on the free movement of such data and repealing Council Framework Decision 2008/977 / JHA.
