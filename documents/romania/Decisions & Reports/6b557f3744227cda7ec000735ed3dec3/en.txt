16.10.2020
Guide adopted by EDPB

At the Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board, held online on 8 October 2020, Guide no. 9/2020 on the concept of relevant and motivated objection from the perspective of the General Regulation on Data Protection (GDPR).
The purpose of this Guide was to clarify the notion of relevant and motivated objection, defined in art. 4 point 24 of the General Regulation on Data Protection, in order to ensure an adequate application in the cooperation mechanism between the national data protection authorities, established by art. 60 of this Regulation.
