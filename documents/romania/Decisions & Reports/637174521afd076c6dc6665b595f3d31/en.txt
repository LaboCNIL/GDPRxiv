Extraordinary plenary of the European Data Protection Board

On November 16, 2018, the Extraordinary Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board took place, a body with legal personality of the European Union, established pursuant to art. 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
One of the main topics was the draft Opinion of the European Data Protection Board on the draft Decision of the European Commission on the recognition of an adequate level of protection in Japan, related to the provisions of art. 45 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
During this Plenary, considering the provisions of art. 70 lit. e) of the General Data Protection Regulation, in order to ensure a coordinated approach of the Member States in analyzing the balance between the right to privacy and the right to freedom of expression, ANSPDCP brought to the attention of the European Committee the proposal to in order to prepare a position paper of the EDPB regarding the balance provided by art. 85 of the Regulation.
