10.03.2022
Press release

Considering the termination of applicability of Government Decision no. 171/2022 regarding the extension of the state of alert on the territory of Romania and the establishment of the measures that are applied during it to prevent and combat the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic, we inform you that starting from March 9, 2022, the program of hearings held at the ANSPDCP headquarters will resume , from Monday to Friday, between 11:00 and 13:00.
