The European Committee for Data Protection, during the February 2023 Plenary, adopted the following guidelines intended to guide the activity of operators and authorized persons:


Guide no. 5/2021 regarding the correlation between the application of art. 3 and the provisions relating to international transfers in Chapter V of the General Data Protection Regulation, final form (after public consultation);


Guide no. 3/2022 on unrecommended patterns in the use of social networks: How to recognize and avoid them, final form (after public consultation);




Guide no. 7/2022 on certification as a transfer instrument, final form (after public consultation).


More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news
