April 2019 plenary of the European Committee
for Data Protection

The Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board, a body with legal personality of the European Union, constituted pursuant to art. 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
At this meeting, the European Data Protection Board adopted Guideline no. 2/2019 on data processing in application of the provisions of art. 6 para. (1) lit. b) of the General Regulation on Data Protection, in the context of online services addressed to data subjects. According to the provisions of art. 6 para. (1) lit. b), the processing is legal if it is necessary for the execution of a contract to which the data subject is a party or to take steps at the request of the data subject before concluding a contract. The adopted document also contains concrete examples of the application in specific situations of this condition of legitimacy of processing.
The guide is in the public consultation phase, in which proposals can be submitted.
