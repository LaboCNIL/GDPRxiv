Plenary of the European Data Protection Board

The Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Committee, a body with legal personality of the European Union, constituted pursuant to art. 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
On this occasion, the Opinion of the European Data Protection Board on the draft Decision of the European Commission on the recognition of an adequate level of protection in Japan was adopted, in accordance with the provisions of art. 45 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
During this Plenary, the Accreditation Guide was also discussed, which aims to establish a common approach in the application of art. 43 of the Regulation.
