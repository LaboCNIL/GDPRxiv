Press release

The National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing issued Decision no. 174/2018 on the list of operations for which it is mandatory to perform the impact assessment on the protection of personal data. It was published in the Official Gazette of Romania no. 919 of October 31, 2018, Part I and entered into force on the date of publication. This decision establishes the list of data processing operations for which operators are obliged to carry out the impact assessment on the protection of personal data.
This decision published on the Authority’s website in the “Legislation / ANSPDCP / Published Decisions” section can also be consulted here.
