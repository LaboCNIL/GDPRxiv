Decision no. 64/2023 - art. 41 GDPR

The National Supervisory Authority for the Processing of Personal Data issued Decision no. 64 of May 17, 2023 regarding the approval of the Accreditation Requirements of a code of conduct monitoring body pursuant to art. 41 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
This was published in the Official Gazette of Romania with number no. 527 of June 14, 2023, Part I and entered into force on the date of publication. Through this decision, pursuant to art. 41 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, the requirements for accreditation of a code of conduct monitoring body.
The decision published on the Authority's website in the "Legislation/ANSPDCP Decisions/Published" section can also be consulted here.
