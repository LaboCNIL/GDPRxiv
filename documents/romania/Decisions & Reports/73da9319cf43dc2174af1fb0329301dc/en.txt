Appointment of the Vice President of the National Supervisory Authority
On April 17, 2019, Mrs. Mirela NISTOROIU was appointed by the Romanian Senate as Vice President of the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing, by Decision no. 19 of April 17, 2019, published in the Official Gazette no. 303 of April 18, 2019.
