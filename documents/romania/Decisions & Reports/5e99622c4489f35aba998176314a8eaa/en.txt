Press release

Given the spread of the Coronavirus epidemic, including in the city of Bucharest, we inform you that, for the prevention of the disease, starting with 10.03.2020 the daily program of hearings held at ANSPDCP will be suspended indefinitely, following that the public relations activity to be carried out daily from 09:00 to 12:00 only by telephone until a date to be determined later.
