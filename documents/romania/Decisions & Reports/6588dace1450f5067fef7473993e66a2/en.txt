Press release

On 25 May 2019, one year shall elapse from the direct application in all Member States of the European Union of the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data. and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC, adopted by the European Parliament and the Council on 27 April 2016.
This Regulation has established a single set of rules designed to protect the privacy of individuals in the territory of the European Union more effectively.
On the occasion of celebrating one year since the application of this European normative act, the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing organizes a series of events in order to increase the degree of informing the general public on the new rules for ensuring the protection of personal data and specific rights enjoyed by individuals.
Thus, to mark this event, with the support of the Bucharest Transport Company STB SA, a message of public interest regarding the main aspects regulated by Regulation (EU) 2016/679 is broadcast on public transport.
