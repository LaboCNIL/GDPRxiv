15.12.2022
Penalty for non-compliance with GDPR

In November 2022, the National Supervisory Authority completed an investigation at the operator Societatea Energetică Electrica S.A. and found the violation of art. 28 para. (3) lit. a) from Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
The operator was penalized for contravention with a fine of 24,654.50 lei, the equivalent of 5,000 EURO.
The investigation was started as a result of the transmission by the operator Societatea Energetică Electrica S.A. of a notification regarding the occurrence of a violation of the confidentiality of personal data, related to the provisions of the General Data Protection Regulation.
In addition to the fine, the operator was recommended to take all the necessary measures to comply with the provisions of art. 46 - 49 of the GDPR.
