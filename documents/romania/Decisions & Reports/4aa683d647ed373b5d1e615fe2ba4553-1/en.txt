During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, held online on April 6, 2022, a Declaration of this body was adopted regarding the announcement of the agreement in principle between the European Commission and the USA regarding the conclusion of the new Trans-Atlantic Agreement on Data Protection.
Also, on the occasion of the same meeting, a Letter to the European Commission was adopted, regarding the need to ensure the independence of the national data protection authority in Belgium, in relation to certain legislative changes expected internally.
