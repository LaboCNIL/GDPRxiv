A new fine based on the RGPD

On 28.10.2019, the National Supervisory Authority completed an investigation with the operator FAN COURIER EXPRESS SRL and found that it violated the provisions of art. 32 para. (1) and para. (2) of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC (RGPD).
The operator FAN COURIER EXPRESS SRL was sanctioned with a fine in the amount of 52,325.9 lei, the equivalent of 11000 EURO.
The sanction was applied to the operator because it did not implement adequate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk of processing generated in particular, accidentally or illegally, by destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or unauthorized access to data personally transmitted, stored or otherwise processed, which led to the loss of personal data (name, surname, card number, security card code (cvv), cardholder address, personal numeric code, serial number and identity card , IBAN account number, approved credit limit, mailing address) and the unauthorized disclosure / access of personal data, being affected by security incidents a number of approximately 1100 individuals concerned, although the operator had the obligation to take adequate security measures personal data according to the provisions of art. 5 para. (1) lit. f of the RGPD.
