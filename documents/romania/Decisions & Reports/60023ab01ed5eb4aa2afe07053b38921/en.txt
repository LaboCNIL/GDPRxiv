RGPD fine

The National Supervisory Authority has completed an investigation at the operator BNP Paribas Personal Finance SA Paris Bucharest Branch (CETELEM IFN S.A.), finding the violation of the provisions of art. 12 para. (3) of the General Data Protection Regulation.
The operator of BNP Paribas Personal Finance SA was sanctioned with a fine of 9508 lei, the equivalent of 2000 EURO.
The investigation was started as a result of some complaints claiming that the operator did not respond to the petitioner within the term provided by art. 12 para. (3) of the General Data Protection Regulation, although it had requested the deletion of certain personal data reported in the Credit Bureau's record system.
In accordance with art. 12 para. (3) of the General Data Protection Regulation, the controller has the obligation to respond to the requests of data subjects without undue delay and at the latest within one month from the receipt of the request.
Also, a corrective measure was applied to the BNP Paribas Personal Finance SA operator, which consisted in adopting measures, at the company level, regarding the settlement of the requests of the persons concerned, so that in all cases, the provisions of art. 12 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
