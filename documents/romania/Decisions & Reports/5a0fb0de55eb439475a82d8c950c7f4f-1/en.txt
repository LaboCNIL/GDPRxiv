During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection which took place on September 12-13, 2022, the Declaration on the project of the European Code on police cooperation, proposed by the European Commission, which aims to strengthen cooperation in the field, was mainly adopted .
In this context, we mention that, after the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection in July, during which the mandatory Decision no. 2/2022, based on art. 65 GDPR, regarding the draft decision on Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Instagram), the Data Protection Authority of Ireland adopted the final decision by which it imposed a fine of 405 million euros.
