October 2019 plenary of the EDPB

The Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board (body with legal personality of the European Union, established under Article 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation) took place on 8-9 October 2019, during which they were mainly adopted. , the following documents:

 Guide on the legality of contract-based processing in the case of online services - art. 6 para. (1) lit. b) GDPR

The Committee adopted the final form of this guide to clarify the applicability of Art. 6 para (1) lit. b) of the General Regulation on Data Protection (GDPR) in the context of information society services. It contains both general aspects regarding the possibility of using this condition of personal data processing in the case of the execution of a contract, as well as numerous edifying examples useful in the activity of the operators. Also, situations are presented in which the applicability of art. 6 para. (1) lit. b) interferes with other grounds of legality of the processing provided by art. 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation.

 Opinion on the draft decision regarding Equinix Common Corporate Rules (BCR), pursuant to art. 64 GDPR

Following a request from the UK Supervisory Authority, the European Data Protection Supervisor adopted an opinion stating that the Equinix Joint Corporate Rules contain the necessary elements and safeguards under Art. 47 GDPR.
