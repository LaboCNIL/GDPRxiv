During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection, held online on December 14, 2021, the final form of Guideline no. 1/2021 on examples of security breaches.
This document aims to support operators and authorized persons, being complementary to the Guidelines on the notification of security incidents, adopted in 2017.
Guide no. 1/2021 contains numerous concrete edifying cases, related to the processing risks generated in particular by the nature of the data, their volume and the number of affected persons, including examples related to the impact of human errors by operators' employees, as well as recommendations for risk prevention.
