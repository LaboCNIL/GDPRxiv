20.01.2021
Plenary EDPB

The following documents were mainly adopted at the Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board, held online on 14 January 2021:

 Guide to examples of security breaches;
 The guide regarding the application of art. 62 GDPR (on joint operations of national supervisory authorities);
 Joint opinion of EDPB and EDPS on the implementation of the draft European Commission Decision on the standard contractual clauses between the operator and the proxy (referred to in Article 28 paragraph 7 of the GDPR)
 Joint opinion no. 1/2020 of EDPB and EDPS on standard contractual clauses for transfer to third countries.

In this context, we note that the main documents adopted at the previous Plenary in December consisted of:

 Guide no. 10/2020 regarding the restrictions regulated by art. 23 GDPR;
 Guide no. 2/2020 regarding the application of art. 46 para. (2) letter a) and art. 46 para. (3) lit. b) regarding the transfer of personal data to public authorities from third countries;
 Guide no. 6/2020 on the interaction between the Second Payment Services Directive and the GDPR.
