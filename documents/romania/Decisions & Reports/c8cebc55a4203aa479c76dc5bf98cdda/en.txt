Press release

Having regard to the application, from 25 May 2018, of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC (General Data Protection Regulation), our institution issued Decision no. 99/2018 regarding the cessation of the applicability of some normative acts of administrative character issued in application of Law no. 677/2001 for the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, applicable from May 25, 2018 and published in the Official Gazette of Romania with no. 432 of May 22, 2018.
This decision, which was published on May 23, 2018 on the Authority's website in the section Secondary Legislation / ANSPDCP Decisions, can also be consulted here.
