Another fine for violating the RGPD

The National Supervisory Authority completed on November 19, 2019 an investigation at the operator Globus Score SRL (former Instrumental Theory SRL), as a result of a notification and found a violation of the provisions of art. 83 para. (5) lit. e) of the General Regulation on Data Protection, reported in art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) and corroborated with art. 8 of O.G. no. 2/2001.
The operator Globus Score SRL was sanctioned with a fine of 9551,800 lei, the equivalent of 2000 EURO.
As the operator did not respond to the requests of our institution, he was initially sanctioned with a warning for violating art. 58 para. (1) lit. a) and letter e) of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
The corrective measure was also applied to the operator to transmit to the supervisory authority, in writing, all the requested information, within 5 working days from the communication of the minutes.
As the operator Globus Score SRL did not carry out the corrective measure ordered by the National Supervisory Authority, it was sanctioned with a fine.
At the same time, the corrective measure was applied to the operator to transmit to the Authority, in writing, all the requested information.
