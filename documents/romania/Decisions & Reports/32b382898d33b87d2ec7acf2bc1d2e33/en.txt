June 2019 plenary of the European Data Protection Board

On June 4, 2019, the Plenary of the European Data Protection Committee, a body with legal personality of the European Union, constituted pursuant to art. 68 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
At this meeting, the European Data Protection Board adopted:

 the final version of Guide no. 1/2019 on codes of conduct;


 the final version of the Annex to Guide no. 4/2018 on the accreditation of certification bodies;


 the final version of the Annex to Guide no. 1/2018 on certification.

More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news/news_en
