14.05.2021
Fine for GDPR violation

The National Supervisory Authority completed an investigation of a natural person and found a violation of the provisions of art. 5 para. (1) letter a) and b) and paragraph (2), related to art. 6 paragraph (1), as well as the provisions of art. 13 par.(1)-(3) and art. 32 paragraph (2) of the General Data Protection Regulation.
The natural person, as an operator, was fined for contravention in the total amount of 974.89 (equivalent to the amount of 200 EURO).
The investigation was started as a result of receiving several reports complaining that through the website https://declaratieppr.ro, by filling in a form that generates a self-responsible declaration necessary to leave the home during the state of emergency process certain personal data, namely name, surname, parents' surname, domicile, personal numerical code, identity document series and number, actual home address, place of travel, purpose of travel and signature.
In the course of the investigation, the National Supervisory Authority found that the operator did not present evidence to show that it legally processed the personal data collected and stored on the website https://declaratieppr.ro.
At the same time, it was found that he did not present evidence from which it could be concluded that he had informed the data subjects about the processing of their personal data, collected on the same website.
Also, the operator (natural person) did not take adequate security measures to ensure that the file containing personal data of the data subjects is not subject to processing risks, generated in particular, accidentally or illegally, by destruction, loss , modification, unauthorized disclosure or unauthorized access to personal data transmitted, stored or otherwise processed.
