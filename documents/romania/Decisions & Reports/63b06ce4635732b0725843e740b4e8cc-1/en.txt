20.10.2020
Another fine for GDPR violation

On September 15, 2020, the National Supervisory Authority completed an investigation at the operator Globus Score SRL and found that it did not carry out the corrective measure ordered to provide the information requested by the Supervisory Authority, thus violating the instructions of our institution, act provided for in art. 83 para. (5) lit. e), related to art. 58 para. (1) of the General Data Protection Regulation.
As such, the operator Globus Score SRL was penalized for contravention with a fine of 9,713.14 lei, the equivalent of 2000 EURO.
At the same time, the operator was also given the corrective measure of submitting all requested information to the supervisory authority.
In this context, we specify that art. 83 para. (5) lit. e) from the General Regulation on Data Protection, provides the following:
"(5) For violations of the following provisions, in accordance with paragraph (2), administrative fines of up to EUR 20,000,000 or, in the case of an enterprise, up to 4% of the total annual worldwide turnover corresponding to the financial year shall be applied previously, taking into account the highest value:
e) failure to comply with an order or a temporary or definitive limitation on processing, or suspension of data flows, issued by the supervisory authority pursuant to Article 58 paragraph (2), or failure to grant access, in violation of Article 58 paragraph (1)."
