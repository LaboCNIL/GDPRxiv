EDPB plenary – December 2022

During the two Plenary Sessions of the European Data Protection Committee in December 2022, three Decisions were adopted regarding the relevant objections regarding the draft decision of DPA Ireland regarding Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (Meta IE) – Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp Ireland platforms Limited, pursuant to art. 65 para. (1) letter (a) of the GDPR.
A Declaration was also adopted regarding the implications of Decision C-817/19 of the European Court of Justice of the EU regarding the implementation of Directive (EU) 2018/681 regarding the use of PNR data in the member states.
