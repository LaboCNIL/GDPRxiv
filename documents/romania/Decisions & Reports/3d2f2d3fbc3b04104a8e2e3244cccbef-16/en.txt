22.12.2020
Penalty for GDPR violation

The National Supervisory Authority for the Processing of Personal Data completed on 20.11.2020 an investigation at S.C. C&V Water Control S.A., to which he imposed a fine, as a result of the fact that this operator did not provide the information requested by our institution, thus violating the provisions of art. 83 para. (5) lit. e) correlated with the provisions of art. 58 para. (1) lit. (a) and (e) and art. 58 para. (2) lit. i) from the General Regulation on Data Protection.
As such, the operator S.C. C&V Water Control S.A. was penalized for contravention with a fine of 9746 lei, the equivalent of 2000 EURO.
At the same time, the operator was also given the corrective measure of transmitting to our institution all the information requested through the previous addresses.
