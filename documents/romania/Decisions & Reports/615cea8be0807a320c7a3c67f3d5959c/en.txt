PRESS RELEASE

On the occasion of the celebration of the European Data Protection Day, on January 28, 2019, the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing organizes the Conference, entitled "Ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation and applicable national regulations", which will take place on Parliament Palace, Human Rights Hall (1st floor), Monday, January 28, 2019, 1:00 p.m.
For the entrance of the guests to the event, the access gate located opposite the Local Police will be used, towards the C1 entrance of the Chamber of Deputies (from Constitution Square).
Accredited media representatives will have access to the Human Rights Hall through the A3S2 entrance of the Chamber of Deputies.
