During the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection that took place in October 2022, the Declaration on the choice of the digital euro from the perspective of data protection was adopted, also related to the intention to prepare a European regulation for the introduction of the euro digital currency.
Also, a List of procedural aspects proposed to be harmonized at the European Union level was adopted, in order to ensure the effectiveness of the application of the General Data Protection Regulation.
