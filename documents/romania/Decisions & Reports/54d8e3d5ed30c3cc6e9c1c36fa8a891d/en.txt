Online drawing contest for children up to 14 years old

The National Supervisory Authority is organizing an online drawing contest for children up to the age of 14, with the theme: “What does personal data / protection of personal data mean” on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of May 25, 2020? to which Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC (General Data Protection Regulation) has become applicable.
The contest is addressed to children up to 14 years old, the registration for the contest will be made by the parent / guardian / legal representative of the minor participating in the contest by sending his drawing from his e-mail address with his consent, until on May 28, 2020, at 12.00.
Please consult "Information for the processing of personal data for registration in the competition organized by ANSPDCP on the occasion of 2 years of RGPD"
The drawings can be sent to the e-mail address dedicated to the contest: desene@dataprotection.ro.
The winner of the contest will be nominated on June 2, 2020 and will receive a diploma, medal, stick, agenda and ANS promotional materials.
The winner will also be able to visit the headquarters of the National Supervisory Authority, at a later date to be determined by mutual agreement.
