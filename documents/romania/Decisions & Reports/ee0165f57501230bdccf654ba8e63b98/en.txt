November 2019 plenary of the EDPB

The Plenary Session of the European Data Protection Board took place on 12-13 November 2019, during which the following documents were mainly adopted:

 Guide no. 3/2018 on the territorial applicability of the General Regulation on Data Protection (art. 3) - final form, after public consultation

The Committee adopted the final form of this guide to clarify the applicability of Art. 3 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), taking into account the proposals received during the public consultation stage. This instrument contains numerous examples, being intended to ensure a unitary interpretation and application of the provisions of art. 3 of the GDPR at the level of all Member States, in various situations.

 Guide no. 4/2019 on Art. 25 Data protection by design and by default - version for public consultation

The adopted guide contains important aspects regarding the interpretation and effective application of the principles of privacy by design and by default, highlighting the obligations of operators to take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure effective compliance with the principles of personal data processing and rights of data subjects. This document also presents operational examples in the specific context of certain processing.
This Guide is open to the public for 8 weeks, during which proposals and comments may be submitted.
The third EU-US Shield of Privacy Shield Assessment Report was also approved.
More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news/news_en
