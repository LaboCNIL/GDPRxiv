10.03.2022
Press release

Considering the termination of applicability of Government Decision no. 171/2022 regarding the extension of the state of alert on the territory of Romania and the establishment of the measures that are applied during it to prevent and combat the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic, we inform you that starting from March 9, 2022, the work schedule at the registry of the ANSPDCP resumes , from Monday to Friday, between 9.00 and 13.00.
