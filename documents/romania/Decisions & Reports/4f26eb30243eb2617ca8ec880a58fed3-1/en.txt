The February 2020 plenary session of the EDPB

During February 18-19, 2020, the Plenary of the European Committee for Data Protection took place, during which the following documents were mainly adopted:

 The Committee's contribution regarding the evaluation of the application of the European Regulation on Data Protection, related to art. 97
 The guide on the transfer of data between public authorities under the conditions of art. 46 para. (2) lit. a) and art. 46 para. (3) lit. b) from the European Data Protection Regulation

The guide is subject to public consultation.
More information is available at: https://edpb.europa.eu/news/news_en
