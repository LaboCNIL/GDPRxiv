Press release

On 25 May 2019, one year shall elapse from the direct application in all Member States of the European Union of the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data. and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC, adopted by the European Parliament and the Council on 27 April 2016.
On the occasion of celebrating one year since the application of this European normative act, the National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing will organize on May 24, 2019, at the headquarters, a debate in which representatives of professional associations will participate.
The event aims to provide an opportunity for debates on the application of the provisions of the General Regulation on data protection, Law no. 129/2018, of Law no. 190/2018 on some measures for the implementation of the General Regulation on data protection and Decisions issued by the National Supervisory Authority.
Issues of practical applicability will be addressed, in particular with regard to the data protection officer, ensuring the rights of data subjects, assessing the impact on data protection, codes of conduct and notifying the breach of personal data security.
