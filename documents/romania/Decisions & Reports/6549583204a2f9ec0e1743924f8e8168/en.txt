Press release

Given the spread of the Coronavirus epidemic, including in Bucharest and the declaration of a state of emergency, we inform you that, for the prevention of the disease, starting with 17.03.2020, the ANSPDCP registration program will be reduced indefinitely, and it will be it takes place only on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays, between 9.00 and 11.00, until a later date.
