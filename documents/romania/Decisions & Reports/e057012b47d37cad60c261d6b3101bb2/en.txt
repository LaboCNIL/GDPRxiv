OPEN DAY - May 25, 2018

The National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing organizes the “Open Day” to mark the application, starting with May 25, 2018, of the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC, adopted by the European Parliament and the Council on 27 April 2016.
On this occasion, during Friday, May 25, 2018, between 11:00 and 13:00, we invite persons interested in the field of personal data protection to the headquarters of our institution in B-dul Magheru no. 28-30, et. 5, cam. 3.
You will have the opportunity to visit the areas open to the public on this occasion and to receive general information on the application of the New General Data Protection Regulation and on the specific activity of the National Supervisory Authority.
