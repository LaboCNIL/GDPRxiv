ANSPDCP decision - Accreditation requirements of a code of conduct monitoring body pursuant to art. 41 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679
The draft Decision on the approval of the Accreditation Requirements of a code of conduct monitoring body pursuant to art. 41 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 is subject to public debate in accordance with Law no. 52/2003 on decision-making transparency in public administration, republished, with subsequent amendments and additions, being accessible here.
The requirements for accreditation of a code of conduct monitoring body pursuant to art. 41 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 were submitted for opinion to the European Committee for Data Protection, which issued Opinion no. 3/2023, in accordance with art. 64 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679,
Proposals, suggestions and opinions of interested persons can be sent to the e-mail address anspdcp@dataprotection.ro, within 10 calendar days from the date of publication of the announcement.
