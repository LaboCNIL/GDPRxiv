Deliberation HABS-2023-002 of May 11, 2023Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des LibertésNature of the deliberation: Internal provision cnilLegal status: In force Date of publication on Légifrance: Thursday May 25, 2023NOR: CNIL2313442XDeliberation n° HABS-2023-002 of 11 May 2023 empowering agents of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties to draw up a report pursuant to the fifth paragraph of Article 22-1 of Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 as amendedThe National Commission for information technology and freedoms,
Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of April 27, 2016 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data;
Having regard to Directive (EU) 2016/680 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data by competent authorities for the purposes of prevention and detection of criminal offences, investigations and prosecutions in this area or the execution of criminal penalties, and the free movement of such data;
Having regard to law no. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its articles 10 and 22-1;
Having regard to decree no. 2019-536 of May 29, 2019 as amended, taken for the application of the law of January 6, 1978, in particular its articles 41, 45-1 and 45-2;
After having heard Mr. Benjamin TOUZANNE, Government Commissioner, in his observations, Decides: and those appointed pursuant to the first paragraph of Article 41 of Decree No. 2019-536 of May 29, 2019 as amended, are authorized to draw up a report pursuant to the fifth paragraph of Article 22-1 of the Law of January 6 1978 amended:
