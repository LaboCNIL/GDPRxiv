Deliberation 2018-264 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 Générale" presented by the firm BRM AvocatsDeliberation n° 2018-264 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR training entitled "Formation Informatique et Libertés Générale" presented by the firm BRM Avocats (Request n° 18004405)The National Commission for IT and freedoms, Seizure by the firm BRM Avocats, on February 27, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning its GDPR training entitled "Computer Training and General Freedoms"; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of individuals with regard to the automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of April 27, 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Having regard to decree no. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Having regard to deliberation n° 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the CNIL, in particular its articles 32 and following; Having regard to the deliberation n° 2017-220 of July 13, 2017 modifying the reference system for the issuance of labels in terms of training relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data; Having regard to the file and its additions; On the proposal of Mr. Maurice RONAI, Commissioner, Chairman of the Committee labeling and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or to procedures aimed at the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data". The training, subject of the request, is composed as follows: Definitions; Founding principles; Rights of individuals; The data protection officer. label application submitted by BRM Avocats includes the following additional module: presentation of the role of the data protection officer. rules of procedure. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL "RGPD training" label for the training entitled "Computer Training and General Freedoms" of the firm BRM Avocats. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
