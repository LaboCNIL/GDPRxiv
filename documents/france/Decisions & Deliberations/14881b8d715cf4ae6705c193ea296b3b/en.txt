Deliberation 2018-269 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 in the field of health" presented by the firm VIGIER AvocatsDeliberation n° 2018-269 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR training entitled "Protection of personal data in the field of health" presented by the firm VIGIER Avocats (Request no. ° 18006610) The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seizure by the firm VIGIER Avocats, on March 26, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning its GDPR training entitled "Protection of personal data in the field of health "Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of individuals with regard to automatic processing of personal data Personal; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Having regard to decree n° 2005- 1309 of October 20, 2005 amended taken for the application of law n ° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Considering the deliberation n ° 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the rules of procedure of the CNIL, in particular its articles 32 and following; Considering the deliberation n ° 2017-220 of July 13, 2017 modifying the reference system for the issuance of labels in terms of training relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing personal data; Considering the file and its supplements; On the proposal of Mr. Jean-Luc VIVET, Commissioner, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the law of 6 January 1978 amended provides that the CNIL "issues a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data". The training, subject of the request, is composed as follows: Definitions and principles;Material and geographical scope;Conditions of lawfulness;Rights of the persons concerned;The data protection officer;The framework for processing in the field of health.The label application submitted by VIGIER Avocats includes the following additional modules: presentation of the role of the data protection officer; presentation of the framework for processing in the field of health. The Commission recognizes that the format ion presented complies with the standard to which it relates, in accordance with article 37 of its internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL "GDPR training" label for the training entitled "Protection of personal data in the field of health" of the VIGIER Avocats firm. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
