Deliberation 2018-278 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 » presented by the company ANAXILDeliberation n° 2018-278 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR training entitled "Training DPO/DPD" presented by the company ANAXIL (Request n° 18006956)The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seizure by the company ANAXIL, on March 30, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning its GDPR training entitled "DPO/DPD Training"; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of persons at with regard to the automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing personal data and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Considering the decree n° 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 modified taken for the application of the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the CNIL, in particular articles 32 and following; in terms of training relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data; Considering the file and its supplements; On the proposal of Mr. Jean-Luc VIVET, commissioner, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Ms. Nacima BELKACEM, Commissioner of the Gov erment, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of persons with regard to the processing of personal data". The training, subject of the request, is composed as follows: The basics of personal data protection; The basics of compliance in the context of personal data protection; Two protagonists of compliance in the context of the protection of personal data. The label application submitted by the company ANAXIL includes the following additional modules: presentation of the CNIL and its missions; presentation of the formalities prior to the implementation of the processing; presentation of the framework for data transfers outside the European Union; presentation of the role of the data protection officer; presentation of the framework for processing within the field of health; presentation of the CNIL's a posteriori control power; presentation of the CNIL's sanctioning power; presentation of the penal provisions associated with non-compliance with the European General Data Protection Regulation. The Commission recognizes that the training presented complies with the reference system to which it relates, in accordance with article 37 of its internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL label "RGPD training" for the training entitled "DPO/DPD training" of the company ANAXIL. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
