Deliberation 2020-030 of January 31, 2020Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des LibertésNature of the deliberation: Members and agentsLegal status: In force Date of publication on Légifrance: Thursday March 12, 2020NOR: CNIL2006380Deliberation n° 2020-030 of January 31, 2020 relating to the election of the president of the college of members of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties responsible for the indirect exercise of rightsThe National Commission for Computing and Liberties,
Considering the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 modified relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its articles 52, 108 and 118;
Having regard to decree n° 2019-539 of May 29, 2019 taken for the application of law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its articles 141 to 143;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2019-016 of February 21, 2019 appointing the members of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties responsible for the indirect exercise of the rights of the persons concerned provided for in Articles 41, 42 and 70-22 of the law of January 6, 1978 modified; After resignation of Mr. Marc DANDELOT of his functions of president of the college of the members of the national Commission of data processing and freedoms in charge of the indirect exercise of the rights;
After having proceeded on January 31, 2020 to a vote under the conditions set by article 71 of the rules of procedure,
FindingArticle 1Ms. Sophie LAMBREMON, honorary adviser to the Court of Cassation, is elected president of the college of members of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties responsible for the indirect exercise of rights.Article 2This deliberation will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic.
The president,
