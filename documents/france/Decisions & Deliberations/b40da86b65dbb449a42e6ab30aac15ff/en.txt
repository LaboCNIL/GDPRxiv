The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Considering law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to computing, files and liberties, in particular its article 12;
Having regard to law n° 2017-55 of January 20, 2017 as amended on the general status of independent administrative authorities and independent public authorities, in particular its article 14;
Having regard to decree n° 2019-536 of May 29, 2019 as amended, taken for the application of law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms;
Having regard to Decree No. 2020-173 of February 27, 2020 relating to the methods of remuneration of members of independent administrative authorities and independent public authorities, in particular its article 6;
Having regard to the decree of February 27, 2020 taken pursuant to decree n° 2020-173 of February 27, 2020 relating to the methods of remuneration of the members of the independent administrative authorities and the independent public authorities, in particular its articles 4 to 6;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2020-057 of May 28, 2020 amending the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties; After having heard Mrs. Marie-Laure DENIS, President, in her report, and Mr. Benjamin TOUZANNE, Government Commissioner, in his observations, Decides: Article 1I. - Before the first sentence of the first paragraph of Article 80 of the internal regulations of the National Commission for Data Processing and Liberties, the following sentence is added: The fixed compensation allocated to the president of the restricted formation is set by the Order of February 27, 2020 issued pursuant to Decree No. 2020-173 of February 27, 2020. II. - After article 80 of the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties, an article 80 bis is inserted as follows: Art. 80a. - Vacation allowances allocated to the chairman of the restricted committee or to one of the members of the restricted committee ruling under the simplified procedure.
The amount of compensation allocated to the chairman of the restricted body or to one of the members acting alone under the procedure provided for in article 22-1 of the amended law of 6 January 1978 (simplified procedure) is set at 50 euros per file.
When, within the framework of this procedure, the respondent asks to be heard and a meeting is organized to hear his oral observations, the indemnity allocated to the chairman of the restricted committee or to one of its members is supplemented. a fixed indemnity of 50 euros per file. Article 2 The President and the Secretary General of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties are responsible, each as far as he is concerned, for the execution of this decision, which will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic.
