Deliberation 2018-267 of June 21, 2018Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des LibertésLegal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018Deliberation n° 2018-267 of June 21, 2018 labeling the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company Ubisoft EntertainmentDeliberation no. 2018-267 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company Ubisoft Entertainment (Request no. 18005212)The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seized by the company Ubisoft Entertainment, on March 02, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning the compliance of its governance procedure with the General European Regulation on Data Protection; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of persons at regard to the automated processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 relating for the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Having regard to decree no. 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 as amended, taken for the application of law no. January 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Having regard to deliberation n° 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the CNIL, in particular articles 32 and following; Having regard to deliberation n° 2017-219 of July 13, 2017 amending the reference system for issuing labels in terms of governance procedure to ensure data protection; Having regard to the file and its supplements; On the proposal of Mr. Jean-Luc VIVET, commissioner, member of the Committee of labeling and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima B ELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of persons with with regard to the processing of personal data". The Commission acknowledges that the governance procedure relating to the General Data Protection Regulation presented complies with the reference system to which it relates, in accordance with Article 37 of the internal regulations. Resolves To issue the CNIL "GDPR Governance" label for the governance procedure to ensure data protection within Ubisoft Entertainment. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the internal regulations of the Commission. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. The organization undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at t erm of the first year.The PresidentI. FALQUE-PIERROTIN
