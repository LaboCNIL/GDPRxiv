Deliberation 2018-272 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 Deliberation No. 2018-272 of June 21, 2018 labeling the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company Cadre de VieDeliberation no. 2018-272 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company Cadre de Vie (Request no. 18006713)The National Commission for Computing and Freedoms, Seized by the company Cadre Life, on March 28, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning the compliance of its governance procedure with the General European Regulation on Data Protection; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of persons with regard to the automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of gifts born physical with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 as amended relating to data processing , to files and to freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Considering the decree n ° 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 modified taken for the application of the law n ° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to the data, files and freedoms; Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the CNIL's internal regulations, in particular articles 32 and following; Having regard to deliberation no. 2017-219 of July 13, 2017 on modification of the reference system for the issuance of labels in terms of governance procedure aimed at ensuring data protection; Having regard to the file and its additions; On the proposal of Mr. Maurice RONAI, Commissioner, Chairman of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the comments by Ms. Nacima BELKACEM, commissioner of the G government, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of persons with regard to the processing of personal data". The Commission acknowledges that the governance procedure relating to the General Data Protection Regulation presented complies with the reference system to which it relates, in accordance with Article 37 of the internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL label "Governance RGPD" for the governance procedure tending to ensure the protection of data within the company Cadre de Vie. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
