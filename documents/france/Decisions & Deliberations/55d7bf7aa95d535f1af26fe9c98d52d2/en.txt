Deliberation 2019-013 of February 21, 2019National Commission for Computing and LibertiesNature of the deliberation: Members and agentsLegal status: In force Date of publication on Légifrance: Saturday March 09, 2019NOR: CNIX1906168XDeliberation No. 2019-013 of February 21, 2019 on the election the president, the vice-president and members of the restricted formation of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties
The National Commission for Computing and Liberties,
Considering the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 modified relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 13;
Considering the decree n° 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 modified taken for the application of the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 70;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 as amended adopting the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties, in particular its article 16;
After proceeding to the vote, in the presence of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner,
Finding: Article 1
Mr. Philippe-Pierre CABOURDIN, Mrs. Anne DEBET, Mrs. Sylvie LEMMET, Mr. Alexandre LINDEN and Mrs. Christine MAUGÜE are elected members of the restricted formation of the commission.Article 2
Mr Alexandre LINDEN is elected chairman of the restricted committee. Article 3
Mr. Philippe-Pierre CABOURDIN is elected vice-president of the restricted formation of the commission.Article 4
The restricted formation of the committee is thus composed of:
Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, President.
Mr. Philippe-Pierre CABOURDIN, vice-president.
Mrs Anne DEBET.
Mr. Philippe GOSSELIN.
Mrs Sylvie LEMMET.
Ms. Christine MAUGÜE.Article 5
This deliberation will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic.
The president,
M. L. Denis
