Deliberation 2018-274 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 : a global, practical and tooled approach (software)” presented by the company BMI SYSTEMDeliberation n° 2018-274 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR training entitled “Protection of personal data: a global, practical and tooled approach (software) " presented by the company BMI SYSTEM (Request No. 18006849) The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seized by the company BMI SYSTEM, on March 29, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning its GDPR training entitled " Protection of personal data: a global, practical and equipped approach (software)”; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of persons with regard to the automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data personal nature and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11 -3° c); Having regard to Decree No. 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 as amended, taken for the application of Law No. 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the CNIL, in particular articles 32 and following; training relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data; Considering the file and its supplements; On the proposal of Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, Commissioner, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "issues a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data". training, subject of the request, is composed as follows: What is the protection of personal data? ;The rights of the persons concerned;Transfers of data outside the European Union;Accountability, the processing register and the impact study.The label application submitted by the company BMI SYSTEM includes the following additional module: presentation of the framework for data transfers outside the European Union. The Commission acknowledges that the training presented complies with the reference framework to which it relates, in accordance with Article 37 of its internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL "RGPD training" label for the training entitled "Protection of personal data: a global, practical and tooled approach (software)" from the company BMI SYSTEM. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
