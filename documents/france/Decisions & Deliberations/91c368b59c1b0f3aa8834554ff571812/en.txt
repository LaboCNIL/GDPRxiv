Deliberation 2018-266 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 Deliberation No. 2018-266 of June 21, 2018 labeling the GDPR governance procedure presented by the group of public interest Santé Informatique BretagneDeliberation n° 2018-266 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the RGPD governance procedure presented by the public interest group Santé Informatique Bretagne (Request n° 18004608)The National Commission for IT and freedoms, Seizure by the public interest group Santé Informatique Bretagne, on March 03, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning the compliance of its governance procedure with the General European Regulation on Data Protection; Having regard to Agreement No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of individuals with regard to automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Reg Lement (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/ 46/CE; Having regard to law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Having regard to decree n° 2005-1309 of October 20 2005 amended taken for the application of law n ° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Considering the deliberation n ° 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the internal regulations of the CNIL, in particular its articles 32 and following; Considering the deliberation n ° 2017-219 of July 13, 2017 modifying the reference system for the issuance of labels in terms of governance procedure tending to ensure data protection; Considering the file and its supplements ;On the proposal of Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, curator, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or procedures aimed at protecting individuals with regard to the processing of personal data". The Commission acknowledges that the governance procedure relating to the General Data Protection Regulation presented complies with the reference system to which it relates, in accordance with article 37 of the internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL "Governance RGPD" label for the governance procedure tending to ensure the protection of data within the public interest group Santé Informatique Bretagne. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
