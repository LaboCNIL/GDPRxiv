As part of these checks, they are responsible for having the necessary modifications and deletions made and for determining, in agreement with the controller, whether the result of the checks carried out and, where applicable, the data in question, can be communicated to the applicant. Article 3 This deliberation will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic. President Marie-Laure DENIS
