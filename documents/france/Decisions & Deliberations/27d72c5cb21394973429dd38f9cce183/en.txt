Deliberation 2018-270 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 Deliberation No. 2018-270 of June 21, 2018 labeling the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company IvoxeDeliberation No. 2018-270 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the GDPR governance procedure presented by the company Ivoxe (Request No. 18006620)The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seized by the company Ivoxe, March 27, 2018 , a request for the issuance of a label concerning the compliance of its governance procedure with the European General Data Protection Regulation; Having regard to Convention No. 108 of the Council of Europe for the protection of individuals with regard to the processing automated processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data at the ard of the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3° c); Considering the decree n ° 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 modified taken for the application of the law n ° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the CNIL's internal regulations, in particular articles 32 and following; Having regard to deliberation no. 2017-219 of July 13, 2017 amending the reference system for issuance of labels in terms of governance procedure aimed at ensuring data protection; Having regard to the file and its supplements; On the proposal of Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, commissioner, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM , Government Commissioner, Formula I he following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the amended law of January 6, 1978 provides that the CNIL "delivers a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data. of a personal nature". The Commission acknowledges that the governance procedure relating to the General Data Protection Regulation presented complies with the reference system to which it relates, in accordance with Article 37 of the internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL label "GDPR Governance" for the governance procedure to ensure data protection within the Ivoxe company. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
