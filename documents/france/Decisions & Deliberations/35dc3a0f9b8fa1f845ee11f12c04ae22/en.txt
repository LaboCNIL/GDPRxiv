Deliberation 2021-102 of September 9, 2021Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des LibertésNature of the deliberation: Members and agentsLegal status: In force Date of publication on Légifrance: Wednesday September 15, 2021NOR: CNIL2127501XDeliberation n° 2021-102 of September 9, 2021 on the election the Deputy Vice-President of the National Commission for Computing and LibertiesThe National Commission for Computing and Liberties,
Considering the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 modified relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 9;
Having regard to decree n° 2019-536 of May 29, 2019 as amended, taken for the application of law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 4;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 as amended adopting the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties, in particular its article 16;
Having proceeded to the vote, Notes: Article 1
Ms Sophie LAMBREMON is elected Deputy Vice-President of the Commission.Article 2
The office of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties is thus composed of:
Ms. Marie-Laure DENIS, President;
Ms. Sophie LAMBREMON, Deputy Vice-President;
Mr. François PELLEGRINI, vice-president.Article 3
This deliberation will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic.
The president,
M. L. Denis
