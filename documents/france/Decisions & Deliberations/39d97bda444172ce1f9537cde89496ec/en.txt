Deliberation 2021-101 of September 9, 2021Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des LibertésNature of the deliberation: Members and agentsLegal status: In force Date of publication on Légifrance: Wednesday September 15, 2021NOR: CNIL2127499XDeliberation n° 2021-101 of September 9, 2021 on the election two members of the restricted formation of the National Commission for Computing and LibertiesThe National Commission for Computing and Liberties,
Considering the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 modified relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 9;
Having regard to decree n° 2019-536 of May 29, 2019 as amended, taken for the application of law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 6;
Having regard to deliberation no. 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 as amended adopting the internal regulations of the National Commission for Computing and Liberties, in particular its article 16;
After having proceeded to the vote, Finds: Article 1 Mrs Anne DEBET and Mr Bertrand DU MARAIS are elected members of the restricted formation of the Commission. Article 2 The restricted formation of the National Commission of data processing and freedoms is thus composed of:
Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, President;
Mr. Philippe-Pierre CABOURDIN, vice-president;
Mr. Alain DRU;
Mrs. Anne DEBET;
Mr. Bertrand DU MARAIS;
Mme Christine MAUGÜE.Article 3This deliberation will be published in the Official Journal of the French Republic.The President,
M. L. Denis
