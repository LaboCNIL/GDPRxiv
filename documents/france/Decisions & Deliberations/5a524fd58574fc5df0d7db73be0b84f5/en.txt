Deliberation 2018-279 of June 21, 2018 National Commission for Computing and Liberties Legal status: Repealed Date of publication on Légifrance: Friday July 20, 2018 Deliberation No. 2018-279 of June 21, 2018 labeling the GDPR governance procedure presented by the Agency for Mutualization of Universities and Higher Education EstablishmentsDeliberation n° 2018-279 of June 21, 2018 on the labeling of the RGPD governance procedure presented by the Agency for Mutualization of Universities and Higher Education Establishments (Request n° 18007138)The National Commission for Computing and Liberties, Seizure by the Agency for Mutualization of Universities and Higher Education Establishments, on March 30, 2018, of a request for the issuance of a label concerning the compliance of its governance procedure with the European Regulation General on Data Protection; Having regard to Council of Europe Convention No. 108 for the Protection of Individuals with regard to the automatic processing of personal data; Having regard to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of physical data with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC; Having regard to law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 as amended relating to data processing, files and freedoms, in particular its article 11-3 ° c); Considering the decree n° 2005-1309 of October 20, 2005 modified taken for the application of the law n° 78-17 of January 6, 1978 relating to data processing, files and freedoms; Considering the deliberation n ° 2013-175 of July 4, 2013 adopting the CNIL's internal regulations, in particular articles 32 and following; governance to ensure data protection; Having regard to the file and its additions; On the proposal of Mr. Alexandre LINDEN, Commissioner, member of the Labeling Committee and after having heard the observations of Mrs. Nacima BELKACEM, Government Commissioner, Makes the following observations: Article 11-3° c) of the law of January 6, 1978 amended provides that the CNIL "issues a label to products or procedures aimed at the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data". The Commission recognizes that the governance procedure relating to the General Data Protection Regulations presented complies with the reference framework to which it relates, in accordance with Article 37 of the internal regulations. Decides On the issuance of the CNIL "GDPR Governance" label for the governance procedure aimed at ensuring data protection within the Agency for Mutualization of Universities and Higher Education Establishments. This label is issued for a period of three years in accordance with Article 39 of the Commission's internal regulations. The use of the LABEL CNIL mark is subject to compliance with the rules for the use of the collective mark. undertakes to provide the CNIL with an activity report at the end of the first year.The PresidentI.
